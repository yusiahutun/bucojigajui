The Snyder women stayed inside the home throughout the program, but Dale and Izzy had a cordial conversation while sitting down outside, and Izzy was able to hold his own when Stacy’s father spoke. “The whole Love is Blind thing I get that,” Dale says. “But love also needs to eat.
