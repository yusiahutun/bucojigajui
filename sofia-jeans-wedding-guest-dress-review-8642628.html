I'm Packing This Flattering $26 Wedding Guest Dress for My Trip to Italy

Publish date: 2024-10-03

I’ve attended a significant number of weddings in the past two years and have easily spent hundreds of dollars on dresses—some I’ve only worn once. Honestly, my wallet is tired, so I’m currently in my strict budget era. With this new mindset and a friend’s wedding quickly approaching, I set out on a mission to find a flattering and affordable dress that I could wear more than once this summer.  

So, I turned to one of my favorite hidden gem retailers to look for a breezy, appropriate dress—Walmart. I’m happy to report that I found the perfect little number that checks all of the boxes in the Sofia Jeans by Sofia Vergara Tie-Front Maxi Dress, and it was just $26. It’s safe to say my mind was blown. 

Sofia Jeans by Sofia Vergara Tie-Front Cutout Maxi Dress

As a curvier woman with a larger bust (36C), I was hesitant about the $26 dress' fit, but I was so impressed with the size range and on-point measurements. The green floral-patterned maxi is available in sizes XS through 5X, and the lightweight fabric is perfect for warm weather. It isn’t lined, but the dress isn’t see-through, and I enjoy the airiness since I tend to run hot. Plus, the adjustable straps are supportive enough that I don’t need to wear a bra. 

The patterned fabric has a crepe-like finish that packs well and doesn’t show wrinkles, meaning I’ll also take it with me on my trip to Italy this summer with zero fear that it’ll need to be ironed or steamed upon my arrival.  The tie-front detail is flattering, and the stretchy back hugs my body without leaving me feeling constricted. While the tiered skirt offers an A-line silhouette, it’s not body-hugging around the hips, so I can forgo shapewear underneath. It also has one of my favorite details—pockets. So, when I’m out on the dance floor, I can keep my phone handy to capture special moments. 

Grab my new favorite $26 dress from Walmart while it’s still in stock, and keep scrolling for more shopper-loved styles from Sofia Vergara’s exclusive collection.
