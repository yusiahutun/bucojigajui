Below Deck Mediterranean star FIRED by Bravo for racist post

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Bravo has fired another of its stars for racist actions: this time Below Deck Mediterranean star Peter Hunziker. 

On Wednesday, a Bravo spokesperson confirmed to DailyMail.com that the reality TV star had 'been terminated for his racist [social media] post.'

'Bravo and 51 Minds are editing the show to minimize his appearance for subsequent episodes,' they added. 

Gone: Bravo has fired another of its stars for racist actions: this time Below Deck Mediterranean star Peter Hunziker

The network also made the same statement publicly on their Instagram account. 

A source close to the production also revealed the deckhand's shocking Instagram Story had been brought to the network's attention last week and that Hunziker 'was terminated promptly at that time'.
