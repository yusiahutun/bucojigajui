Kenyans Share Amusing Perceptions about Women Wearing Anklets: &quot;Worse than Pishori Babes&quot;

Publish date: 2024-02-09
  • A debate on whether the anklet is just an accessory or has a deeper meaning raged on social media
  • The discussion, started by NTV host Dr King'ori, attracted many reactions, with numerous women noting it is just a fashion piece
  • However, many men said that they believe women wearing anklets have other hidden agendas

TV personality Dr King’ori has elicited an exciting debate on social media.

Debate on Kenyan women with anklets

On Tuesday, June 15, the NTV show host shared the foot of a woman wearing an anklet and sought to establish the perceptions Kenyans have about them.

"Wadau, naskia haka kakitu pia kako na maneno (I hear this thing has wonders)," he wrote, prompting an outpouring of feedback.
