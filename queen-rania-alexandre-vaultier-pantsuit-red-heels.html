Queen Rania, 52, spellbinds in waist-cinching pantsuit and epic heels

Publish date: 2024-09-29

We're rarely treated to a royal pantsuit moment, especially one fresh off the runway. So when a member of any royal family graced our presence with a two-piece, it's a good day for fashion followers everywhere. Queen Rania Al Abdullah of Jordan just quenched our everlasting thirst for a fresh designer pantsuit, stepping out in Tokyo in her best outfit to date.

On Tuesday, the 52-year-old attended an exhibition at The National Museum of Modern Art in Tokyo, called the '70th Anniversary Exhibition: Secrets of Important Cultural Properties' at the National Museum of Modern Art in Kitanomaru Park.

Part of her royal tour, the cultural trip made for an ideal dress-up opportunity, which Queen Rania took full advantage of. The ever-radiant royal looked divine in a black pantsuit from Alexandre Vaultier's Autumn/Winter 2022 couture collection. Featuring a sculptural fit, a waist-cinching silhouette, classic tailoring, short sleeves and classic button-detailing, the magnificent set looked mesmerising when paired with some cherry red Gianvito Pumps in a pristine satin.

Dior's 'Caro Bag' boasting tulle a black calfskin design coupled with multicoloured pearl detailing added the perfect amount of designer dazzle to her graceful look.

Queen Rania wore her brunette locks down loose in a swish blow-dry and showcased a sun-kissed glamour glow that highlighted her age-defying features.

Upon seeing images of the beloved queen surface online, royal fans were quick to express their adoration for her failproof outfit archive. "She looks like she just got off the runway wow," one wrote, while another commented: "She's just stunning. Love the exaggerated details. She knows what works. This looks amazing!" A third enthusiastically noted: "SLAYYY QUEENNN!" and a fourth agreed, writing: "Fashion icon from head to toe."

As her latest look proved, Queen Rania never ever misses when it comes to serving an outfit that boasts utter chicness. The stylish royal is currently on a tour of Japan with her husband King Abdullah, and her previous tour attire was not to be missed.

During a visit to Emperor Naruhito and Empress Masako at the Tokyo Imperial Palace, the Queen wore a custom beige ‘Robe Manteau’ couture blazer dress from Amsterdam-based designer label, Benchellal. The original Robe Manteau boats a structured wrap around the body, which Rania eschewed in favour of a voluminous collar, matching fabric buttons and elegant bracelet sleeves. The pièce de résistance was a gold bow waist belt from Marni to finish off the customisations that epitomise her signature style.
