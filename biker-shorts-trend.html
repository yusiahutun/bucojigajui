14 Essential Biker Shorts Outfits for Summer

Publish date: 2024-01-28

We have nothing against denim cutoffs or pleated miniskirts, but if we're looking for an easy bottom layer to slip on during the summer, biker shorts are still our go-to. This stretchy, form-fitting staple is super comfortable and not at all fussy, and just like your favorite T-shirt, these babies don't have to be styled casually. When deciding what to wear with biker shorts, the sky is truly the limit, whether rocking them as a cover-up over your swimwear or going full Fancy Grandma with sequins and heels.

Of course, biker shorts outfit ideas are aplenty, and a quick Google search will likely lead you to images of Princess Diana (biker shorts queen!) styling them with a sweatshirt or even a model or influencer, who opted to wear them with a sexy-cute crop top while walking their dog. However, if you're hoping to save time, we've put together our own list of suggestions and tips.

So, if you're ready to take your "warm weather leggings" to the next level, keep reading for our favorite biker shorts outfit combinations.

Wear Your Biker Shorts with a Sweatshirt

There's a reason we're still copying Princess Diana's iconic outfit. This look is classic, comfortable, and uncomplicated — perfect for chilly summer days or transitional weather.

Try Some Basic Layers

A surprisingly sleek combination? Solid biker shorts with a white T-shirt and button-down — especially if you spice things up with kitten heeled sandals and a playful, printed bag.

Keep It Casual in Sneakers

As for which shoes to wear with biker shorts, white sneakers — complete with a chunky pair of socks — will give things a cool '90s vibe. However, if you're hoping for something more breezy, slide sandals are another great and easy option.

Bring Some Edge

If it's a tad chilly outside or you're hoping to toughen up your outfit, chunky boots are a great alternative, while heels will no doubt give your look a dressed-up spin.

Style Your Biker Shorts with an Oversized T-Shirt

One summer outfit you can put together in a flash is an oversized T-shirt and biker shorts with sneakers or sandals. The laid-back pairing is chill enough for various daytime activities, such as a chill brunch with friends or a trip to the beach.

Opt for a Matching Set

Chances are, if you're shopping for biker shorts, the same store sells a T-shirt or sweatshirt to match (see: Good American or Free People). You can also embrace the athleisure trend by rocking a full fitness set, pairing a crop top or bralette with these sporty bottoms.

Try Monochromatic

By now, you know that your top and bottom don't have to match perfectly. If you stick to a single color scheme, your outfit will feel uniform and elevated, no matter which shirt and shorts you wear.

Go the Unexpected Route

Although we typically style biker shorts with basics, the truth is you don't have to keep your outfit simple. Instead, aim to amp things up with voluminous sleeves, sequins, pleated jackets, bow details, hats, and other whimsical items.

Say "Yes" to Crop Tops

If you're hoping to nail the celebrity way of styling biker shorts, a crop top is your best bet. Loose, form-fitting, turtleneck details or cutouts — a great way to spice up this staple and show some skin.

Wear a Blazer with Your Biker Shorts

It's possible to make biker shorts feel pulled-together and professional. Instead of a sweatshirt, shrug on a structured blazer, and if sneakers feel too lax, try loafers instead.

Stay Sexy with Cutouts and Corsets

Since these bottoms are so simple, they'll help balance out the most elaborate, Since these bottoms are so simple, they'll help balance out the most elaborate, outrageous top. Plus, there's no need to stress about which shoes to wear to complete such a bold look; both heels and boots are influencer-approved.

Think Fancy Grandma

Replace your sweatshirt with a cardigan and your sneakers for ballet flats. It's still a comfortable combination, but one that feels a bit more sophisticated and, yes, fancy.

Show Off a Sneak Peek

Ready to reinvent that slip dress or ruffled gown? Let a pair of bike shorts peek out from the side slit. A contrasting color will provide an eye-catching pop.

Use Them for Extra Coverage

If you've ever panicked over the possibility of revealing too much in a short or sheer skirt, a pair of black biker shorts make for a smart and comfortable solution.
