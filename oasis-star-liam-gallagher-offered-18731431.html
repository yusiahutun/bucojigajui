Oasis star Liam Gallagher offered free hair transplants to his fix thinning scalp

Publish date: 2024-05-12

The Wonderwall singer, 41, was approached by hair clinics after his bald spots were snapped in New York.

Liam’s receding locks have forced him to ditch his iconic Mod hairstyle.

But the moody rocker has furiously rejected the hair experts’ offers.

Although he may still have the treatment on his own terms. A source said last night: “Liam has chopped his hair off now because he doesn’t want people to see his uneven patches.

“He is also a bit narked because his older brother Noel still has a sprightly thick head of hair.

“Liam has told his team to flatly reject any offers of a hair growth scheme and won’t even consider doing it for a free trial.

“Some people close to him think he may have some work done but will go public.

“If Oasis do ever get back together then Liam will want to look the part.”

Aussie cricketer Shane Warne, 44, famously boasted of his hair transplant success in a series of adverts. And England football ace Wayne Rooney, 28, said he had one after finding losing his hair in his 20s “stressful”.
