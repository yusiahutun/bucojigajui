The best shoulder length bob braids styles

Is there anything as chic and sophisticated as the bob? It is a timeless hairstyle that has always been in fashion. 1. Off-centre parted shoulder length bob braids

One of the most amazing things about the bob hairstyle is the simple yet significant customizations you can make on your braids. For instance, the deep off-centre part on the shoulder length bob braids makes all the difference. And the good thing is that the centre part makes it easy to keep your strand out of your face.

2. Salt and pepper bob

The salt and pepper bob is for ladies who want to add flavour to their hairstyle. It is a mix of black and grey colours, which you can accessorize as you desire. You can also choose to have the grey colour on one side or mix it with other strands in equal measure. The great thing about this look is that you can never go wrong regarding attire. It easily matches multiple colours.

3. Chunky bob

For a good reason, the chunky bob is a favourite. The hairstyle requires less time to install and looks great. You can achieve this look by sectioning your hair into large box partitions and adding three finger widths of braiding hair to each plait.

4. Pushed back bob braids

The pushed-back bob braids are a stunning, effortlessly cool style that perfectly fits formal and official occasions. Ladies looking for something low maintenance would go for this any time.

5. Half up, half down bob braids

Although the bob look features short braids, that are, at most, shoulder length, you can still do the traditional half-up, half-down look. The tweak will add that extra touch of magnificence to the classic '90s style, which might come out as boring sometimes if not well-customized. And you can elevate it by adding coloured rubber bands for even more nostalgia.

6. Bobbed Fulani braids with fanned ends

Don't you love the bobbed Fulani braids with fanned ends? The style is iconic, which is why many African celebrities love it. For instance, Danai Gurira wore it to the 2019 Oscars and looked elegant.

7. Bob braids with beads

There is so much you can do with short hair braids styles and adding beads is a common tweak. The braids take less time to install and style. Therefore, you will spend less time at the salon. The multicoloured beads add a playful vibe and make you look younger.

8. Bob braids with flipped ends

Braids protect your natural hair; therefore, you can play around however you want. One of the most amazing ways of getting that elegance you have been yearning for in your look is to customize the ends of your bob braids.

The ends are often forgotten, but styling it can bring a good look. You can install beads or colourful wraps on the flipped ends of your bob braids to make them more alluring.

9. Electric blues and hues braided bob

The electric blues and hues braided bob is a chic look. It is perfect for someone interested in trying something outspoken. And the good thing is that they last long. The silver ends placed at the end of each strand bring out the blue's electric hue even more.

10. Short knotless braids bob with a side part

Knotless braids are great. The style takes the protective element a notch higher by not messing up the roots of your hair. You can add jewellery to the ends to give it that edgy feel.

11. Knotted box braids with swooped baby hair

How do you seal the ends of your braids? Did you know that you do not have to do it with water and flame? The knotted box braids with swooped baby hair give a classic example of how to do it without going for the conventional approach of hot water or a flame.

Using rubber bands will still do a great job. And the best thing about rubber bands is that they add style to the look. The result is something that appears unfinished yet elegant.

12. Rolled half updo

You can do so much with braids, including rolling them up. And it is easier to accomplish this look if you have a good base. All you need to do is to divide, twist and pin. It is a great way to style a braided bob for anyone who fancies retro-inspired hairstyles.

13. Long and short bob braids

If you are torn between having long and short bob braids, don’t worry, you can have both. And the solution lies in the long and short bob braids hairstyle. This look has a great asymmetrical twist with long braids, touching the shoulder on one side while the other is shorter. You can add colour, like a couple of purple braids, to make it brighter.

14. Braided and wrapped

The braided and wrapped is your style if you find it hard to leave cornrows behind. The hairstyle starts with cornrows and ends as a freely hanging tight plait, sealed and curled. You can add as many highlights and accents as you desire. Remember, it is all about matching it with your overall look. So, you can style as much as you see fit.

15. Multicoloured bob

The multicoloured bob is fun and vibrant. The style has multiple colours, including purple, light brown, and black. Shaking things up and including conspicuous colours in your hairstyle is always a good idea. You might not love the multiple colours idea, but once you experience the pop, you might stick with the look.

16. Bob topped with a bun

There is little you can do with a bob hairstyle when wrapping. But there is enough volume to create a mid-sized bun, an incredible alternative to the boring half-ponytail. One of the most amazing things about the bun is that it is easy to create, and they open up the face.

17. Cleopatra braids

As you explore new styles, remember Cleopatra braids. The style looks amazing, and it will create fabulous memories. Let the ends loose or seal them with hot water or a flame.

18. Jumbo burgundy braids

The burgundy look never goes out of fashion, and you can have it in your bob braids. It is a great way to add flair to your braids without applying real dye to your natural hair.

19. Asymmetrical bob braids

Among short bob braids hairstyles, asymmetrical bob braids stand out. The most incredible thing about the braid is that it looks stunning with statement jewellery or without accessories at all. Therefore, you can still keep it simple and look fabulous.
