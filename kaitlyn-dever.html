Kaitlyn Rochelle Dever (; born December 21, 1996) is an American actress. She has gained recognition for her roles in the FX crime drama television series Justified (2011–2015), the ABC/Fox sitcom Last Man Standing (2011–2021), the Netflix drama miniseries Unbelievable (2019), and the Hulu drama Dopesick (2021). She earned Golden Globe and Critic’s Choice Award nominations for Unbelievable and Dopesick as well as a Primetime Emmy Award nomination for the latter.
