Lilian Muli Opens Up about Past Relationships, Says Men Dated Her as Trophy Girlfriend

Publish date: 2024-01-27
  • News anchor Lilian Muli disclosed that she felt the guys who dated her or showed interest in her in the past only did so because of her celebrity status
  • According to Muli, most of her dates were mostly held outside the house where they could be seen by people in public
  • The mother of two also said she is an introvert and she felt most people only wanted to be with her based on what they could gain by associating with her

TV journalist Lilian Muli has narrated that most men in her previous relationships only dated her or were interested in her because of her status as a TV celebrity.

Lilian Muli opens up about her past relationships

The mother of two said she was shocked to learn that one of her partners, whom she was constantly being seen with in public, was not really into her but was more interested in what he was gaining by being seen as her lover.
