Floyd Mayweather flaunts customised KSh 18 million Richard Mille watch

Publish date: 2024-08-31

- Floyd Mayweather took to Instagram to tease his followers with the stunning timepiece

- The retired American boxer asserts it is the first of its kind

- The move comes just days after debuting a Rose Gold Limited Edition Arnold Schwarzenegger watch

Floyd Mayweather, retired undefeated boxer, is known for his love for expensive jewellery and loves to splash the cash on them.

After 50 undefeated wins in an amazing boxing career, the 42-year-old enjoys spoiling himself and has overtime shown his love for expensive rides and jewellery.

This is arguably why he has customised one of his watches, Richard Mille RM 011, which he claims is the first one made in the world.

The Richard Mille RM 011 features hours, minutes, seconds, a chronograph function, a 60-minute countdown timer and a 12-hour totalizer, as well as the date and month.

It is valued around KSh 18 million ($168,000), and Mayweather qualifies it as 'the killer'.

The retired boxer took to his Instagram to show off his stunning timepiece and even clarified to his followers on how to acquire it.

"My new, all-baguette, Richard Mille watch is the first one made in the world. If you want this same watch made...get in touch with these two jewelers ASAP. @jadellebh @petermarco90210," he captioned the post.
