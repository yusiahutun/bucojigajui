Lanes of Devils Glen Rd. in Bettendorf closed for water work

Publish date: 2024-05-05

There’s an Our Quad Cities News traffic alert for part of Bettendorf.

Several lanes are closed on Devils Glen Rd. so crews can connect water to a nearby development. Lane closures involve one southbound lane of Devils Glen Rd. and the two-way left turn next to Hy-Vee. All entrances to the store remain open.

Lane closures are expected to last through Saturday, March 23, weather permitting.
