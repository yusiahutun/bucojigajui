Farrah Abraham Shows Off Third Boob Job with Very Revealing Belle Costume

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Farrah Abraham took her new boobs for a spin on Thursday night, hitting her first red carpet since going under the knife for her third breast augmentation.

The "Teen Mom OG" star was spotted at Life & Style's Eye Candy Halloween Party at Riviera 31 in Los Angeles, showing some serious skin in a low-cut Belle costume from "Beauty and the Beast."

"She's a beauty and I think I'm beautiful," Abraham joked when toofab's Candice Brock asked her about the inspiration for her getup. "I just really loved the costume, it's one of my all-time favorite Disney movies."

This was the first time we've seen the 24-year-old reality TV star out in public since her operation on October 16, when she went from 650CC implants to 800CCs and fixing the "severe debilitating pain" she suffered after previous surgeries.

"I'm just happy I'm not in pain anymore," she told us last night. "That's originally why we had the breast reconstruction. I'm happy that I'm able to go out for Halloween, thank god!"

She says she hopes she doesn't end up on the doctor's table again anytime soon -- adding that it's "kind of a rough thing to heal and not go out."

Now that she is back on the town, we couldn't help but notice who joined her for her night out on Thursday: Ex-boyfriend Simon Saran.

"We're back together and you'll see more of that on the 'Teen Mom OG' season coming up," she teased, as her gangster-costumed man stood off to the side.
