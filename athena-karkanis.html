Athena Karkanis Career

She made her screen debut in 2005, in an episode of 1-800-Missing. After that, she has a number of guest-starring, recurring and regular roles on Canadian shows. Before her screen debut, she started with a few voice roles. In 1996, in a TV series Stickin’ Around, she started her voice role. In 2004, in Kevin Hill, she did the voice of Annalisa. Later in the years, she gave her voice to many characters like Diwan in ‘Skyland’, Bream in “Dex Hamilton: Alien Entomologist”, Patty in “Nerdland”, Aviva in “Wild Kratts”, Sidney in “Julius Jr”. Other than giving her voice in tv series, she also had her voice in many video games. A few of her games were “World of Warcraft: Cataclysm”, “Star Wars: The Old Republic”, “Diablo III”, “Heroes Of The Storm”.

Television

Her television roles started in 2004 and she is acting till now. In 2005, she played the role of Annalisa in “Kevin Hill” and the role of Gabrielle in “Kojak”. She was the lead in the 2006 tv series “Growing Up Creepie” and in 2007, she played the main role in “The Best Years”. In 2009, she landed a role in two television films, “U.S Attorney” and “Too Late to Say Goodbye”. In 2010, she had a few small roles in two TV series.

From 2011, she started getting attention and also managed to bag some recurring roles. A few of her shows that year were “Insecurity”, “Covert Affairs”, “Almost Heroes”, “MetJets”. In 2013, she landed a main role in “Low Winter Sun” and “The Lottery”. In 2017, she had the recurring role of Octavia Muss in “The Expanse” and of Abigail Westbrook in “Zoo”. Her last guest role in a TV series was in 2019, in “Corn & Peg”.

Movies

She made her first film appearance in “The Care Bears Big Wish Movie” in 2005. After that her next role was Agent Lindsey Perez in “Saw IV” in 2007 and in 2009 she again played her role in “Saw VI”. She played the role of Heather in 2008’s “The Art of War II: Betrayal”. The same year she also appeared in another movie “Repo! The Genetic Opera”. In 2009, she played a part in the movie “Survival Of The Dead”. She landed the role of Rachel in the 2011 movie “Sacrifice”. In 2012, she also starred in “The Barrens”. In 2013, she also did a short film “Foxed!”. She also did another short movie “Break In Break Out” in 2019.
