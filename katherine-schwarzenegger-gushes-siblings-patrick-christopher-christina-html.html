Katherine Schwarzenegger gushes about her siblings Patrick, Christopher, and Christina

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Katherine Schwarzenegger gushed about her siblings as she posted family photos to her Instagram account on Monday.

The photos captured Katherine, 33, posing alongside her brothers Patrick, 29, Christopher, 25, and Christina Schwarzenegger, 31, all dressed up and looking as happy as can be. 

'I thank God every single day for these humans! I love them SO much and am so grateful for them always ♥️ my forever best friends and siblings. Thanks to @mariashriver and @schwarzenegger,' she captioned the photos.

She tagged their parents, Arnold Schwarzenegger and Maria Shriver, in the caption. The power couple were married for 25 years until their split in 2011. 

They famously remained estranged for years after the journalist filed for divorce in July 1, 2011 following news he fathered a child - Joseph Baena - with their housekeeper Mildred Patricia 'Patty' Baena. Their divorce was at last finalized in 2021.

Not pictured in the photos was Katherine's husband, Chris Pratt, whom she married in 2019.

'You get hit, you fall in love, you get married, now we have two beautiful daughters to add to my family. It feels really great.'

The Jurassic World actor also shares his 10-year-old son Jack with his ex-wife Anna Farris, who he was married to from 2009-2018.
