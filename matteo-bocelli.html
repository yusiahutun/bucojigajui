Matteo Bocelli is a popular Italian singer and musician. Matteo Bocelli is best known as the youngest son of a famous singer, Andrea Bocelli. Moreover, some of his songs include “Tempo”, “Away in a Manger”, “Dime”, “I Miss You Amore”, and many more.

Early Life and Education

Matteo Bocelli was born on October 8, 1997, in Forte Dei Marmi, Lucca, Tuscany. His age is currently 26 years old. He was born to Andrea Bocelli (father) and his first wife Enrica Cenzatti.

His father is also a professional singer and musician. Likewise, he has an elder brother named Amos, born on February 22, 1995. He holds Italian nationality and is of a White ethnic background. According to his birth date, he was born under the zodiac sign Libra.

Regarding his educational qualification, he is a graduate student. He completed his graduation from the Lucca Conservatory in Tuscan.

Career and Professional Life

Professionally, Matteo is a singer and musician. At the Teatro del Silenzio on July 30, 2016, Matteo Bocelli made his stage debut as a tenor in front of 12,000 audiences. He performed arias from Giuseppe Verdi operas alongside singers like Leo Nucci and Sumi Jo. He also performed that year at Celebrity Fight Night’s opening night in Florence’s Cloister of the Basilica di Santa Croce.

Additionally, he performed several romanzas on stage at Palazzo Pitti for “Pitti Uomo 91” on January 12, 2017. In the ‘David Foster and Friends’ competition for the Horatio Alger Award in Washington on March 30, 2017, he gave his first performance in the country. The audience members who were there praised his singing highly.
