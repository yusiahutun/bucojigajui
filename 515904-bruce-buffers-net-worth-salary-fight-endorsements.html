Career in martial arts

Bruce is one of the key personalities in mixed martial arts. He ventured into this industry at 13 when he started studying judo, where he achieved the green belt rank. After his family relocated to Malibu, he was introduced to Tan Soo Do and holds a 2nd-degree black belt.

In his 20s, he started kickboxing but gave up the sport at 32 when he suffered a second concussion. Later, he ventured into UFC announcing, debuting in 1996 during the UFC 8 preliminary fight. Also, he hosted all UFC 10 fights.
