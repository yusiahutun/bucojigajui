Amanda Seyfried's Changing Looks

1995

"I kind of look the same," the Allentown, Pa. native told In Style of this picture. Indeed, at age 10, the budding model was already flaunting long blond mane.

2001

The busy Seyfried finished up her senior year of high school, starred on As the World Turns and still made time for primping: "I used to straighten my hair every day."

2004

The Mean Girls scene-stealer attended the premiere with natural makeup and her bangs pulled back with bobby pins.

2005

The actress wore a rare updo to the Los Angeles Film Festival premiere of Nine Lives.

2006

The small screen star (in both Veronica Mars and Big Love) matched her eye makeup to her temporarily red hair. "It's crazy how eye shadow can totally change your look!" she told In Style.

2008

Seyfried moved into the big time with a role opposite Meryl Streep in Mamma Mia!-and professionally waved locks and bold pink lipstick. "The most glamorous moment of my life," Seyfried said of the premiere. "The makeup, the hair-everything was genius."

2009

Seyfried had glamorous curls for the Academy Awards.

2009

The Jennifer's Body actress got sophisticated with retro curls and mauve lippy. "I don't wear it often," she said of lipstick, "but I like the finish."

2010

If there's one thing Seyfried loves, it's a powerful red lip. "It has a more traditional glamour. It's timeless," she told us.

2010

Now a bankable beauty with Dear John, Chloe and Letters to Juliet, Seyfried branched out with a banded updo and edgy eye makeup.

2011

For the Hollywood premiere of Red Riding Hood, Seyfried tried out a face-framing deconstructed updo.

2011

The bombshell sizzled at the premiere of In Time with a dramatic smoky eye and a creamy pink lip.

2012

For the premiere of Gone, Seyfried wore her flaxen locks in sculpted curls.

2012

At the world premiere of Les Miserables, Seyfried framed her sultry, smoky eye makeup with well-groomed brows.

2013

A teased braid and smoky eye completed the star's Oscar party style.

2013

The actress evoked retro glam for the Lovelace premiere, donning a face-framing chignon and a classic red lip.

2014

Seyfried paired her enviable locks with bronzed makeup at the A Million Ways To Die In The West photocall in London.

2015

With shoulder-length hair and a berry-colored lip, Seyfried looked radiant while attending the K.I.D.S./Fashion Delivers Annual Gala at American Museum of Natural History in New York City.
