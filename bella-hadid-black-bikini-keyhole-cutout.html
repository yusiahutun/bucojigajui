Bella Hadid's Classic Black Bikini Includes a Keyhole Cutout

Publish date: 2024-10-04

It may be the middle of January, but Bella Hadid already has her eye set on summer.

The supermodel embraced the warmer-weather days in her latest Instagram post. She shared a selfie of herself lounging by an outdoor pool and relishing the sun in a stringy black bikini. The bikini top featured a scoop neckline and a wide keyhole cutout in the center while the bottoms included a gold bedazzled hemline and tie-off strings at the sides.

Hadid accessorized her poolside look with some jewelry, including thick gold hoop earrings and layers of delicate gold chain necklaces with a small cross pendant.

It’s not unlike Hadid to share bikini pics in the midst of winter. Last January, she similarly shared a slew of tropical vacation looks from a trip she took to the Cayman Islands.

In one photo dump, the Kin Euphorics partner shared several bikini snaps, including a white triangular two-piece, a floral set with a red-and-yellow smiley-face print, and a brown plaid swimsuit with an underwire bralette top and high-cut bottoms.

More recently, in September, Hadid gave fans a glimpse at her vacation wardrobe. The model enjoyed another girls’ trip to the Cayman Islands with longtime friend Fanny Bourdette-Donon. In photos posted to Instagram, Hadid posed in a strapless black minidress from Buci, which featured a long oval cutout that ended below her belly button, punctuated by a flower-shaped mother-of-pearl button. At the time, she accessorized with a pair of orange beaded bracelets and a black anklet.
