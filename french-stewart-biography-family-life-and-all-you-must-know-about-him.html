French Stewart Biography

Born on February 20, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, Stewart was raised by his mother, a housewife, and his stepfather, a microfilm technician. Stewart had a keen interest in the entertainment industry from an early age, hence his first television appearance at the age of five in a local children’s show called Captain Billy’s Clubhouse. However, he attended Del Norte High School before moving to the American Academy of Dramatic Arts to familiarize himself with the world of acting. He then graduated with a B.F.A. in Dramatic Arts.

More determined to make his breakthrough, Frenchman Stewart patiently toured the regional theater, wore a Yogi Bear costume for seven years at conventions, and fundraising for Hanna-Barbera, before he got his first acting role as a stand-off DJ in the final season of the 1992 American sitcom The New WKRP in Cincinnati, which was a sequel to the original 1978-82 CBS Cincinnati sitcom WKRP. The six-season sitcom 3rd Rock from the Sun actually put Stewart in the spotlight after his role as Harry Solomon in the film. Other television shows he has appeared on recently include Secrets and Lies, 2 Broke Girls, The middle (aired 2013-2017), and many others.
