Khloe Kardashian Sizzles in Skintight Blue Dress for BookCon

Publish date: 2024-10-09

Kim Kardashian may be making headlines for her pregnancy news, but Khloe Kardashian is stealing the spotlight with her killer bod!

The "Keeping Up With the Kardashians" star looked smokin' hot at BookCon in New York City on Sunday, where she was promoting her upcoming advice book.

The 30-year-old bombshell flaunted her enviable curves in a bright blue David Koma bodycon dress with sheer panels, which she paired with nude Christian Louboutin heels, tousled blonde waves and pretty pink lip.

The reality starlet showed love for her glam squad in an Instagram post, writing, "Heading to Book Con in my best blue styled by @monicarosestyle. Wearing David Koma and Christian Louboutin. Glam by @kristoferbuckle and @jenatkinhair."

In April, the youngest Kardashian sister spoke about the upcoming project in a press release via the book's publisher, Regan Arts.

"I am so excited about sharing my philosophy on how I live and the power of strength," she said. "I hope to be an inspiration to readers everywhere on how they can create their own true strength and beauty inside and out."

Khloe has definitely found her beauty, right?!

While Khlo could probably get any guy she wants, it seems she's still talking to her estranged husband, Lamar Odom!

In a teaser clip for the upcoming season of "Keeping Up With the Kardashians," Kim confronts Khloe for staying in contact with him.

"Is that Lamar? You're, like, flirting with him? And called off your whole divorce?" Kim asks her sister. "I just can't take it."
