Emo Night LA Announces The Lineup For Webster Hall Show

Publish date: 2024-04-24

Emo Night L.A.'s T.J. Petracca, Babs Szabo, Morgan Freed (l-r) (photo: Beth Saravo)

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) — Party promoters Emo Night LA revealed the lineup for their upcoming event at the iconic Webster Hall in New York City.

Set for July 26th, the Webster Hall Takeover will feature performances and DJ sets from Kenny Vasoli (The Starting Line), Travis Clark (We The Kings), Craig Owens (Chiodos, BXC), Awsten Knight (Waterparks), Captain Cuts and Dying in Designer.

The event will mark Emo Night LA’s first return to Webster Hall since 2017. Webster Hall just recently reopened after a major refurb that included upgrades to everything from the sound and lighting systems to front of house and backstage amenities, as well as a brand new and much-needed climate control system.

The Webster Hall Takeover is part of a larger tour that will see Emo Night LA produce events around the U.S. If you’re not familiar with Emo Night LA, they produce a popular monthly event in Los Angeles and curated two full-day festivals with Emo Nite Day while also booking spots on lineups at festivals like Life is Beautiful, Slam Dunk, EST Fest, the Vans Warped Tour, Firefly and BUKU in addition to their own independent touring.
