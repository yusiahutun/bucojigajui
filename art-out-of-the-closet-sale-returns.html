Art Out of the Closet Sale Returns

Publish date: 2024-03-08

The beloved Art Out of the Closet sale will return to the Court House Cultural Center in Stuart on October 1 and 2. Shoppers can drop by the renowned event from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. to peruse the donated art treasures out of the Arts Council’s vault. 

Art Out of the Closet supports the recycling and upcycling of art, art supplies, and other arts-related items. Funds raised through the sale of donated items benefit the arts and cultural programming offered at the historic Court House Cultural Center. Donations are accepted year-round, spacing permitting. Those with a donation in mind can email the Art Out of the Closet team at info@martinarts.org.

Donations for the 2021 sale will be accepted through Friday, September 24. Donations after this date will be sold at upcoming Art Out of the Closet sale.
