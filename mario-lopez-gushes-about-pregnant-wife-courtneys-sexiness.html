Take notes, boys! Mario Lopez just proved he’s the most loving hubby to his pregnant wife, Courtney Mazza Lopez, with whom he’s expecting baby No. 3. The TV personality took to Instagram on Wednesday, January 30 to show off his expectant wife as she adorably flaunted her growing baby bump in a leopard print dress. “#WCW [Woman Crush Wednesday] Mrs. Lopez taking her sexiness next level when she’s pregnant,” the 45-year-old captioned the sweet snap.

Fans of the power couple couldn’t contain their excitement over how much Courtney, 36, glowed in the sweet snapshot. “She’s always gorgeous pregnant or not So are you! You guys are the cutest couple,” one fan wrote in the pic’s comments section. “So beautiful inside and out, she is so funny! Congratulations to your entire family Mario!” said another. Take a look at how stunning Courtney looks in Mario’s adorable post below!

Mario and Courtney — who tied the knot in a romantic ceremony in Mexico back in 2012 — share their two kids, Dominic Lopez, 5, and Gia Francesca Lopez, 8. The pair announced in mid-January through an adorable family update video that they are expecting their third child together.

A few days after their exciting news went public, the Saved by the Bell alum gushed to People about expanding his family. In fact, he revealed that he and Courtney have been trying to conceive for quite some time now. “If it didn’t happen, it wasn’t meant to be. Not part of God’s plan. We have one of each,” Mario said of already having one son and one daughter. “But it got in there! It snuck in, towards the end, so a bit of a Christmas miracle.”

Mario also revealed that he and his lady aren’t the only ones thrilled about bringing a little bundle of joy into their lives. “At first they were like, ‘Wait, what?’” he admitted of Gia and Dominic’s reactions. “They couldn’t understand. It took a second for it to register. My daughter got very dramatic. She was like, ‘If I have another brother, I’m telling you right now, I’m moving to Texas!’” Ha!

The TV stud doesn’t seem too worried about the baby’s gender, though. “It doesn’t matter, to be honest, as long as it’s healthy. We’re just very excited,” he gushed. “The gender reveal will be when it’s born!” We wish nothing but the best for Mario, Courtney and their beautiful family!
