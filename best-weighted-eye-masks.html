Best for Travel

Lunya Weighted Silk Sleep Mask

Best for Travel

Lunya Weighted Silk Sleep Mask

Credit: Lunya
Pros
  • Blocks both light and noise.
Cons

In addition to light-blocking eye coverage, Lunya’s silk sleep mask features a comfortable, thick padding that helps muffle noise, too. Inside its casing are recycled glass beads that aim to minimize eye puffiness when chilled. It also nicely comes with a travel pouch for easy storage on the go.

Best Luxe

Saatva Weighted Silk Eye Mask

Best for Comfort

Nodpod Gentle Pressure Sleep Mask

Best for Comfort

Nodpod Gentle Pressure Sleep Mask

Credit: Amazon
Pros
  • Great for side sleepers.
Cons
  • May not stay on all night.

Nodpod’s elastic-free design makes for better weight distribution and an overall more comfortable fit. It’s especially designed for side sleepers, as one side of the mask features a cooling material (that can be put in the freezer), with the other side reserved for warmth.

One satisfied customer writes: “This weighted sleep mask has been a game changer!!!! Not only is it super soft (I love both sides of the material, one is warmer than the other) but it’s just heavy enough to help me fall asleep fast even with other distractions going on, it blocks out all the night.” —MamaBearEst.2021
