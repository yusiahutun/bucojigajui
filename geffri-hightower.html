Geffri Hightower Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Geffri Hightower is a popular American model and actress. Throughout her career, she appeared in numerous television films and programs. She is best known for her performance in the television series All American: Homecoming (2022-23) as Simone Hicks on the CW.

Early Life

Geffri Hightower was born on 25th February 1995 in South Central Los Angeles. She belongs to an African-American family. Geffri does not reveal details of her parents and siblings on the internet. Talking about Geffri’s education, she earned basic education at Alexander Hamilton High School. Later, she enrolled in Mass Communication/Media Studies at Clark Atlanta University in 2011. Later, she graduated and achieved a bachelor’s degree in 2015.

Geffri Hightower Career

Geffri Hightower started her career in the Entertainment industry at a very young age. At the age of nine, she began her Broadway career, playing little Nala in Disney’s The Lion King. Later, she moved her career path from theater to television when she was a teenager.

She has been featured in the television series Jericho (2006) as Allison and in Everybody Hates Chris (2008) as Latrinda. From Sep 2007 – Jan 2010, she worked in the role of “Maya Bennett” in Private Practice. Later, she appeared in short films, including Q & A (2014), Southern Lights Over-Exposed: The Visual Album (2015), and Defending Daddy (2016). Geffri was also recognized for her appearance in series including Finding Carter (2015), The Inspector (2016), Crean X Coffee (2016), Nasty Habits (2017), and Shorts Fired (2017).

Rise to Fame

She was famous after her appearance in the comedy series “The Pre-Quarter Life Crisis (2018)”. In the series, she portrayed the role of Kevin-Rose Hankins along with star cast Taylor Polidore and Donny Carrington. The same year, she seemed in the series WTF, Baron Davis (2018) as Zoe and East of La Brea (2018) as Aisha. After that, she has seemed in the short film, With (out) You (2019), and was recognized as a proficient actress at the LA shorts Fest.

In 2020, she got limelight for her performance as Lydia in the drama “Loco”, which was directed and written by Justin Galindo. The same year, she got the opportunity to join the cast of the sports drama “All American”, created by April Blair. In the drama, she performed the role of Simone Hicks with star cast Daniel Ezra, Greta Onieogou, and Cody Christian. Later, she was cast for the crime drama series “Snowfall” and made her appearance as Khadijah Brown in Season 4 2021 for a few episodes.

Modeling Career

She also worked as a Stylish Fashion Model. As a Model, Geffri worked for Armani beauty, Hervé Léger, JEAN-LOUIS SABAJI. Her work is highlighted in the platform MACRO, AAFCA. She has also been highlighted by Magazine Cosmopolitan. Currently, she worked with Wolf|Kasteler Public Relations, Consulting agency.
