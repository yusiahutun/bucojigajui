Beyonce denies splashing out on 200k shoes

Publish date: 2024-07-25

    Beyonce has denied reports that she purchased a pair of heels for £200,000. The singer was thought to have bought a pair of diamond-encrusted sandals by House of Borgezie for a music video. But her representative has denied the claims, telling that the story is “absolutely not true”.

    The megastar has denied the lavish purchase

    The designer of the shoes had even gave interviews confirming the purchase. "It's the ultimate, Beyoncé's one of those fantasy customers," Christopher told . "If you want someone to model your creations, you can't get any better than Beyoncé."The singer's team was reported to have approached the British label after spotting a pair of their shoes in an article in the American press.

    The heels in questions are diamond-encrusted

    "It's a sale," Christopher revealed. "I've been told the stilettos are for her next video which should be out in the autumn. "The dazzling stilettos in question are encrusted with 1,290 precious stones, with the design inspired by Paul Simon's hit song .The singer was also said to have picked up a gold basque belt worth £55,000 to accessorise what will no doubt be her show-stopping ensemble.

    With her husband Jay-Z

    Beyoncé is no stranger to the world of footwear - she recently teamed up with legendary Italian designer Giuseppe Zanotti to collaborate on a pair of shoes. Giuseppe, 57, revealed that the pair will be 'ultra-thick platform' shoes with a stiletto heel, adding that the pop superstar played an active role in the design process. "Beyoncé has very clear ideas – she knows exactly what she wants," he toldForbes. "The thing which she then also has over other celebrities, is that she has an amazing voice. She's incredibly talented and she's an amazing dancer – she's complete. Few are like Beyoncé."
