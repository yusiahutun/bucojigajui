Apple Martin and Blue Ivy Are Brunch Squad Goals

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Penelope Disick and North West may be winning the mini street style game, but Blue Ivy Carter and Apple Martin are the tots taking over brunch. Apple, the daughter of Gwyneth Paltrow and Coldplay frontman Chris Martin, rang in her 12th birthday on Saturday and celebrated with a sleepover with friends including Blue Ivy. (Meanwhile Beyonce was performing at the Rose Bowl for her Formation tour.) 

The next morning, Paltrow snapped a photo of the girls fresh out of bed and ready for brunch, as a mother with a camera often does. "Birthday brunch squad #godsistersandbesties," she wrote in the caption. 

This isn't Martin and Carter's first hangout, either. They were spending time together back at the Super Bowl, when their parents performed together at the halftime show. 

Consider us jealous.
