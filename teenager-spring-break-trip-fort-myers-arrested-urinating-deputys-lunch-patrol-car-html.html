A teenager on a spring break trip to Fort Myers Beach, Florida was arrested in the early hours of Monday morning after he deliberately urinated in a Lee County deputy’s patrol car and on the deputy’s lunch.

Chris Brennan, 18, was placed in the back of the cop car for his own safety, while the officer interviewed two other people related to an incident at the Pink Shell Resort on Estero Boulevard, Fort Myers Beach at around 1:50 a.m. Monday.

The Lee County deputies were responding to a report of spring breakers smashing bottles on the resort property, according to the police report.

Brennan, 18, was arrested after urinating in the police vehicle and was transported to Lee County Jail

Lee County deputies were responding to a report of spring breakers smashing bottles at the Pink Shell Resort on Estero Boulevard, Fort Myers Beach

Once in the back of the patrol car Brennan began thrashing around, banging his head on the window bars and screaming.

The deputy told him to stop, and Brennan told the deputy ‘I will piss in your car.’

Brennan then urinated through the screen cage in the deputy’s car and on several of the deputy’s personal items, including his cell phone, lunch bag, water bottles and several items issued to him by the sheriff’s office.

Brennan was arrested for resisting an officer without violence and was transported to Lee County Jail, reports Fort Myers News Press.

Fort Myers earned a spot in last year's list of America’s 'Top 10 Trashiest Spring Break Destinations,' the cities college students want to visit most - and their parents want them to visit the least.

Florida had five cities being listed in the top ten, which was compiled by Coedmagazine.com, a lifestyle and entertainment website for college boys.

TOP TEN U.S. TRASHIEST SPRING BREAK DESTINATIONS

1. Las Vegas:  Home to gambling, strip clubs and the only Hooters Hotel. Vegas makes up for not having a beach with its popular pool parties.

2. Key West, Florida: Highlights include Garden of Eden, a clothing-optional bar.

3. South Padre Island, Texas: Coed says 'How can it get any trashier than South Padre, a beach that essentially exists for the sole purpose of having wild parties?'

4. Daytona, Florida:  This city’s high number of underage-drinking arrests helped win it a spot on Coed’s list.

5. Myrtle Beach, S.C: Coed makes a distinction between North and South Myrtle Beach and advises that if it is trashiness you’re after, South Myrtle beach is where you want to be.

6. Miami Beach: The online magazine points out that The Jersey Shore taped a season here.

7. Panama City Beach, Florida:  Coed, again, uses the reality to show to measure the trash factor, saying that stars from the Jersey Shore were among last year’s celebrity visitors.

8. Fort Myers, Florida: Lana Kai hosts a daily 'booty contest' during high season.

9. Lake Havasu, Arizona:  A tattoo convention is held during Spring Break. According to the site, Kokomo Havasu and Martini Bay are two of the trashier destinations.

10. San Diego:  'We know that most of the people who live in the San Diego area head to other destinations for Spring Break,' Coed writes, 'but that just means the people left partying here are extra trashy.'
