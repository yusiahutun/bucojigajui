Scotty T SPLITS from girlfriend Chloe Elizabeth after cryptic post: 'Not good enough'

Publish date: 2024-09-19

The Geordie Shore hunk is one man we never thought would settle down with a girl, but he found romance with makeup artist Chloe Elizabeth Wilson.

Sharing cute snaps of the pair together, they looked happy throughout their relationship, but it seems it wasn’t all plain sailing for the couple.

Speculation started earlier this month after Chloe stopped posting snaps with her former beau, and fans were questioning their split on a photo from last month.

Although the pair stayed quiet for a little while, Chloe took to Instagram to confirm the split on her story.

She wrote: "Thank you for all the messages. What’s happened has happened.

"Sometimes you just can’t be good enough for someone, but it’s not the end of the world.

"I’m sure I’ll find my human one day."

As people started trying to guess whether they’d split prior to the announcement, one user wrote on the old snap: "What’s happened between you two?"

While another added: "Has anyone noticed she didn’t get anything from him for Valentine’s Day and she hasn’t posted about how much she loves him?"

Scott is yet to say anything on their split, and Chloe hasn’t gone into too much detail about the reason behind the breakup.

The pair first took to Instagram in December to confirm the official relationship, after Scott posted a snap of the brunette beauty posing in a thong on his Instagram story.

Their romance came a year after Scott’s split from his previous girlfriend Francesca Toole in 2017 – and the hunk has proven he’s happy to be tied down.

Although his romance with Chloe hasn’t gone the distance, it’s unlikely the hunk is going to stop looking for love just yet.

He’s come a long way from his old Geordie Shore days where he would go on the pull with his co-star Gaz Beadle.

Now that Gaz is in a long term relationship and has his son Chester, it’s only fitting that Scotty will settle down soon.
