Singer-Songwriter Carson Wallace Signs With WME

Publish date: 2024-03-30

Carson Wallace

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) — Rising Nashville-based country singer-songwriter Carson Wallace has signed with WME for global booking representation.

Carson, who relocated to Nashville from his native California, has accumulated more than a million streams with his debut singles “Lonely Look Like” and “Ghost In My Chevy”.

“Carson’s voice and songwriting talent immediately caught our attention. It’s an honor to be a part of his team and have him at WME,” said WME’s Carrie Murphy.

“Carrie, Braeden, and the entire WME team were some of the earliest believers in myself and my artistry. I couldn’t be more excited and honored to be working together!” Wallace added.

Wallace’s signing with WME follows his recently announced publishing deal with Sony Music Publishing and HARDY’s Relative Music Group. He is managed by Anthony DeVincenzo and Enzo DeVincenzo at Red Light Management.
