Lewis Naked Bus Controversy

Lewis and fellow comedian Jim Norton was arrested in 2000 for their involvement with The Naked Teen Voyeur Bus. The bus had acrylic glass walls and contained numerous young topless women. The bus was driven around Manhattan in the same route that was used by then-President Bill Clinton on that day. Black was released twenty-eight hours after his arrest.
