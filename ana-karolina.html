Early Life

Ana Karolina was born on October 10, 1996, in Sao Paolo, Brazil. Ana has not shared much information about her childhood. Karolina went to a local high school. She received a degree in Environmental Management from the State University in Sao Paolo, Brazil. Karolina was good in her studies and has always been an excellent student.

Ana Karolina Career

Ana Karolina graduated with a degree in Environmental Management but she had other plans for her future. After her graduation, she started modeling. The glamorous world of modeling always fascinated her. She decided to make a career in modeling.

Ana started taking up modeling assignments. Initially, she would get small parts in brand campaigns. Later she got the opportunity to collaborate with many brands and businesses. She quickly gained a lot of followers. She would endorse brands on her Instagram page. Ana is also passionate about environmental studies and environmental issues.

She uses her social media handle to create awareness about the issues. Her degree in Environmental Management comes in handy from time to time. Ana is now a full-time social media influencer. She has around 25.5k followers on Instagram. Her followers are her audience for all her brand’s endorsement assignments.
