Publish date: 2024-09-18

Steven Ferdman//Getty Images

It would have been difficult to miss the fact that Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas tied the knot this weekend. The A-list couple put on a three-day wedding extravaganza in India and documented much of the celebrations on Instagram, which included a sneak peek at two of her wedding dresses.

For her first outfit, Chopra chose a very colourful traditional Indian dress as she celebrated the Mehendi, which is a pre-wedding ritual.

Related Story

For her second dress, the actress went for something completely different to the first. She wore a silver sequin sari for the Sangeet, where both families put on musical performances.

She accessorised with a statement diamond choker necklace and beautiful sparkling bangles, with her long hair styled straight.

Ralph Lauren has confirmed that the house was responsible for another of Chopra's dresses, presumably a traditional Western ensemble, which she wore during the Christian ceremony on Saturday. Hopefully we will get a glimpse of it soon.
