Los Lonely Boys Cancels Upcoming Performances After JoJo Garza Exits The Group

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Los Lonely Boys

(CelebrityAccess) — ‘Texican’ group Los Lonely Boys announced the cancellation of all remaining tour dates through 2019 after bassist and vocalist Jojo Garza announced he was exiting the group.

Garza is a founding member of Los Lonely Boys and one of three brothers who make up the group, which emerged from the Central music scene in the mid-1990s.

In a statement on their social media, a rep for the group said: “JoJo is stepping away from touring with Los Lonely Boys. Subsequently, the remaining dates this year for the band are being canceled.”

The post also hinted that Los Lonely Boys might be parting ways, adding “We need time to decide what directions our musical careers will take without our brother and musical partner.”

The post went on to thank their the group’s fans for their support, “We apologize for these canceled shows. We are honored to have been able to perform as brothers and family for so many years for our fans worldwide. The pure love that our fans have given to each of us has always touched our hearts. We are so grateful.”
