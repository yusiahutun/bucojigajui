Who Is Scheana Shay's Fiance, Brock Davies?

Publish date: 2024-06-04

"Vanderpump Rules" star Scheana Shay and Brock Davies are engaged. According to Life & Style, Brock was born in New Zealand but grew up in Australia. He boasts an impressive physique, honed by years of playing professional rugby. In fact, he emigrated to the United States to pursue an athletic career. He now owns two F45 training studios and travels the world with Scheana.

The couple welcomed baby Summer Moon Honey Davies on April 26, 2021. He shared the news on Instagram and said, "April 26th was already a special day but yesterday I watched @scheana birth our beautiful baby girl with sooo much love." Brock also has two older kids, Winter and Eli, who live in Australia with their mother, per Life & Style.

When some fans attacked him for showing off his youngest and asked him "where are your other kids," the gentle giant reached out and kept it 100. "I have been separated from my two kids for the [last] 6 years. And in this time they have been raised in Australia with their mum and step dad," Brock said on Instagram. "During our separation my relationship with the kids mother has been tested and I didn't make the best decisions back then. So I am actively working to be a better farther [sic] for both summer and my two back in Australia." Good on you Brock! Here's to the happy couple and, potentially, their blended family.
