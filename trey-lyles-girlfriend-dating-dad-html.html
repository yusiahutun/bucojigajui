Does Trey Lyles Have A Girlfriend? A Look At His Dating Status

Publish date: 2024-05-29

Trey Lyles is an American basketball player who plays for San Antonio Spurs for the 2019-20 NBA season. After being traded on 12th July 2019 from the Denver Nuggets, he has been playing as the power forward for the Spurs.

For those unaware, Trey has played for three teams in the NBA since his recruitment in the 2015 NBA Draft. He was selected by Utah Jazz and played for two seasons with Jazz before moving on to Denver Nuggets. Trey played for the same amount of time in Nuggets as well before being traded for the second time to San Antonio Spurs.

Does Trey Have A Girlfriend?

Trey’s relationship status is not clear as of December 2019. He is not seen going out with anyone in public, and neither is linked with anyone in the media that could be considered his significant other.
