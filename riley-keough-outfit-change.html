Riley Keough's Outfit Change in From Late Night TV to Premiere

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Every time we see Riley Keough in a new role, I’m once again shocked by her range. So imagine my surprise when she stunts on us wearing two different vibes in a matter of hours. Riley Keough’s outfit change from her appearance on Jimmy Kimmel to her appearance on the red carpet for the premiere of “Under The Bridge” has Daisy Jones-heads well fed.

Keough seemed to have started her day at Jimmy Kimmel Live wearing a very sober, serious medium brown tweed dress that stopped at the knees. She paired it with tortoiseshell sunglasses and a pair of heels. The dress has two square pockets at the breast, extra long sleeves, and a major poofy shoulder moment.

Later, she was seen wearing a very sexy, very sheer black dress that showed a black bra and and underwear, one of our favorite trends of the summer. She styles the look in the same heels as her daytime look. Her black midi dress plays a lot with textures, ruffles, micropleats, and seams. Her dress has major Daisy Jones all grown up vibes. It’s sexy, but also elegant and classic.
