Vineboxs 12 Nights of Wine Advent Calendar Is Now Available to Ship

Publish date: 2024-05-31

If the sun setting earlier and earlier is bumming you out, an advent calendar is a surefire way to brighten your evening—especially if each day means a serving of delicious wine. Vinebox’s 12 Nights of Wine is by far one of the most popular seasonal calendars, having sold out seven years in a row thus far. It’s now available for shipping, and we expect the limited quantity to go fast.

The advent calendar will put you on an “exclusive journey into the world of fine wines” with its hand-picked selections from popular wine regions such as American wine country (California), Italy, France and Spain. 

Shoppers can choose from three different curations, such as the Cozy Collection which comes with 12 different reds perfect for “fireplace chats and wool blankets” or the Chill Collection that caters to those who prefer a mix of crisp whites, rosés and chillable reds. You can also go all out and gift yourself both for 24 nights of delectable wine.

While the calendar may not come with hidden windows to unbox each day, the all-new flat-box unwrapping concept displays all 12 individual servings of wine in a very interesting way. Any of the calendars would also make for the perfect gift for the upcoming holiday season to those seeking to sample an assortment of wines.

There’s no predicting when this popular advent calendar will sell out again, so make sure to purchase one (or both) before they’re gone.
