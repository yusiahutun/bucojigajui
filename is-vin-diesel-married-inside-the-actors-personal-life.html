Is Vin Diesel married? Inside the Actor's Personal Life

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Despite his devotion to family values, fans do not know if famous action star Vin Diesel is married or not.

Over the years, Vin Diesel has built a reputation for being one of the biggest action stars in Hollywood. The actor’s role as Dominic Torreto in the “Fast and Furious” franchise has made him a household name in the industry. However, one theme that fans have come to understand as Diesel’s motto is family. 

Throughout his appearance in the “Fast and Furious” franchise, Diesel has never shied away from detailing how important family is to him. His speeches about family, unity, and love became even more prominent when he lost his closest friend and co-star Paul Walker. However, despite this, fans of the action star have noticed that although Diesel speaks about family, he rarely ever mentions his own most importantly his wife. 

Unlike some actors who keep their life off social media, Diesel is one of the most active actors on Instagram. Despite this, the actor mainly posts pictures of himself and those of the “Fast and Furious” cast. Diesel rarely mentions his wife. The actor’s reluctance to talk about the subject has inspired many people to ask if Vin Diesel has tied the knot. 

Is Vin Diesel Married?

According to reports, Diesel is a dedicated family man who adheres to what he preaches about the importance of family. The “Fast and Furious” star is reportedly in a relationship with long-term partner Paloma Jiménez. As per reports the couple has stayed together for more than 15 years and in 2007, Diesel went public with their relationship.

Still, despite their many years together and even becoming parents, Diesel, and his partner are not married to one another. While this might seem surprising to some however the actor had never walked down the aisle with anyone in his life. Diesel’s decision not to have married Jiménez yet after so many years together is a mystery many people are surprised about. 

Diesel and Jiménez Are Parents to Three Children

Although they are not legally married, Diesel and Jiménez are parents to three adorable kids. The couple welcomed their first child Hania Riley Sinclair in 2008. The little girl now 14 years old made her acting debut in 2019 after appearing in the film. “Fast and Furious Spy Racers. 

Meanwhile, the couple’s second child Vincent Sinclair is a carbon copy of his father. The youngster who is just 12 years old has followed in his father’s footsteps and  made his acting debut in “Furious 9.” In the film, Vincent played a younger version of his after who consistently appeared in flashbacks.

Who is Paloma Jiménez? 

Diesel’s partner Paloma Jiménez Denagustin is a model and actress born in Acapulco, Mexico. Jiménez moved to the U.S. with the dreams of expanding her career and had featured in magazines and famous brands like Coca-Cola, Honda, and Pantene. 

Diesel and Jiménez’s relationship started in 2007, however, the couple has kept key details about their romance a secret. Still, according to some reports, the duo allegedly met through mutual friends. Despite the actor’s reluctance to speak about their romance, Diesel told Parade in 2008 he took his time to find a partner.

Before his relationship with Jimenez, reports linked Diesel with his “Fast and Furious” co-star Michelle Rodriguez. The duo who portrayed lovers on-screen became superstars overnight and they also pursued a relationship off-screen. However, the duo could not keep their offscreen relationship as long as their on-screen romance.  

What Happened Between Diesel and Michelle Rodriguez?

While fans have witnessed some of their favorite stars pursue romantic relationships unsuccessfully and some end badly, Rodriguez and Diesel’s romance only ran its course. During an interview with USA Today Rodriguez lauded Diesel’s attitude and personality explaining he’s not a jerk. However, she mentioned his raspy voice was an attribute that got her to fall for him. Although, the duo has since moved on with other people their on-screen “Fast and Furious” romance lives on.
