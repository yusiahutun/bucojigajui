Model's tiny 'jeankini' denim shorts has fans asking 'where's the rest of them?'

Publish date: 2024-08-18

A model has baffled the internet after showcasing her toned, slim legs in a pair of pants so short that viewers called them a "jeankini".

Gabriella Ellyse, an OnlyFans model from the US, has shared several videos of the minuscule denim shorts to her TikTok account.

In one clip seen more than 1.2 million times, she dons a beige sleeveless top and denim shorts almost in the shape of a thong and dances with her dog outside her home.

The garment barely covers her thighs but Gabriella insists: "Normalising these bottoms."

TikTok mum Heather Cole was one of the thousands to watch the clip and was absolutely stunned by what she saw.

In a stitch video, she says: "I have a genuine question thought, why are those? Are they like jean underwear?

"Jean short-short shorts? Jean pants? Do you make them? Do you buy them? So many questions...I don't understand.

"Where do they come from? The pants section or the underwear? I'm confused."

And Heather was not the only one feeling confused, many viewers also struggled to understand where she found the garment.

One suggested: "They are like thongs, but in denim...I'm just going to call it 'jhongs'."

A second wrote: "What is the point of these shorts I'm confused. Jeans+bikini bottoms...jeankini?"

"What happened to the rest of the shorts?" a third asked. "Did you buy them at 98% off?"

Gabriella hit back at the comments and posted more outfits with different pairs of shorts, all about the same length.
