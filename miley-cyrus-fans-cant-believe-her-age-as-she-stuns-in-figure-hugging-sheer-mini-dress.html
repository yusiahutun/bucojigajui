Miley Cyrus fans 'can't believe her age' as she stuns in figure-hugging sheer mini dress

Publish date: 2024-03-30

Miley Cyrus is celebrating her birthday today, and some fans, who have been following her since her Disney days, can’t believe her age.

The music star first rose to fame when she was cast as the lead in the Disney series, Hannah Montanna in 2005, at 13 years old.

Miley Cyrus stuns in 31st birthday pics – and fans can’t believe her age

Taking to her Twitter and Instagram, the newly turned 31-year-old posted a range of pics from her big day, as she stunned in a black mini dress with matching gloves.

The bandeaux corset dress, which is sheer on the top half, shows off the star’s curves, and her tattoos as the top half of her arms are left bare.

To mix things up, the Flowers singer accessorized the look with a leopard skin bag. Letting the world know her age, she captioned one of the posts: “31.”

In another post, the singer revealed her birthday gift to herself, performing for herself and singing her favorite songs.

Within this, was her first live performance of her song Flowers.

As fans rushed to the comments to compliment the star, some couldn’t believe she was turning 31.

“Can’t believe Hannah turns 31 today,” wrote one.

Another said: “The fact she is only 31 but has lived so many lives…ohh the day we get a Miley Cyrus documentary, I’ll be so seated!”

Dolly Parton reveals how she became Miley’s godmother

Aside from her famous dad, Billy Ray Cyrus, Miley also has a famous godmother, music legend Dolly Parton.

The 77-year-old recently revealed how she became The Climb singer’s godmother in an interview with Howard Stern on his Sirius XM show.

Billy Ray toured with Dolly while promoting his 1992 single, Achy Breaky Heart, and the two became pretty darn close.

The story was simple really. “He said … ‘We’re having a girl, and you’ve got to be her godmother!’ And I said, ‘Well, I’d be honored.’ So actually, Miley and I took over from there. We just stayed very, very close through the years,” the Jolene singer recalled.
