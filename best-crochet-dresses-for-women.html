13 best crochet dresses for your 2023 vacation: From H&amp;M to ASOS, M&amp;S &amp; more

Publish date: 2024-10-08

Crochet dresses are one of this summer's hottest trends, and the flattering style is here to stay. Stylish and comfortable, a crochet dress is a must-have for any summer wardrobe, and we've seen every celebrity rocking the look, from Stacey Solomon to Maya Jama, Dua Lipa and more. 

How to style a crochet dress

Not only is a crochet dress an effortlessly stylish choice for holidays and summer days out, the knitted look is a hit for festival season - and teamed with a pair of cowboy boots, you have the ultimate 'cool girl' ensemble. The versatile style can be worn with just about any footwear, from chunky sandals to white trainers.

How we chose the best crochet dresses

  • Price: I've selected crochet dresses with a range of price points, from affordable high street to luxury. 
  • Variety: From mini dresses to slinky maxi dresses, I've selected a variety of styles to suit plenty of preferences and occasions. 
  • Trusted brands: All the brands I've chosen are trusted brands I, or members of the HELLO! team, swear by. We've not tried on all of the products but the brands are some of our favourites, so the quality should be high.
