Where is A.J. Lee today?

AJ, who is known for her fashionable style in the ring, retired in 2015. The wrestler always came out with attires that made her fans watch her fights. She opted to concentrate on being an author after giving up wrestling.

Her memoirs were released in April 2017. Crazy Is My Superpower: How I Won by Breaking Bones, Breaking Hearts, and Breaking the Rules was the book's title. It was a huge success and a New York Times best seller.

Since then, AJ has said that writing is "very much the next chapter" of her life and that the drafting of her second book has already started. It was announced in December 2017 that Crazy Is My Superpower was being turned into a TV show, with AJ serving as the show's executive producer and writer.
