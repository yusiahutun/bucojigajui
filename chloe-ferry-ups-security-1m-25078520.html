Chloe Ferry ups security on 1m mansion after being left 'traumatised' by trespasser

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Geordie Shore babe Chloe Ferry has revealed she moved to her Newcastle mansion after becoming sick of fans turning up outside her house in the middle of the night.

She eventually decided to move out and head to a more private different area but once more, she failed to retain a level of privacy at her home.

Chloe, 26, has spoken exclusively to the Daily Star to explain how she has been coping since became the victim of trespassing last month after claiming a man broke into the grounds of her pad.

The MTV star was forced to call the police after being left traumatised following a break-in at her new million-pound property.

The reality star said: "I've changed the whole of my gates because this person actually climbed over the to get into my house. I had a trespasser on my land.

"I know they probably didn't mean any harm but the whole reason I moved to this area was for privacy and a lot of people would come outside my house and take pictures at night time.

"Imagine being asleep and waking up to that, it's quite scary."

The alleged trespasser left Chloe a note underneath her plant pot and took photos on the grounds before making a sharp exit.

She continued: "I know people probably don't mean any harm but it's quite scary, your home is your home you want to feel safe don't you?"

When asked if she would consider moving again, Chloe batted away the idea saying she could never afford to currently.

She added: "I wouldn't be able to afford to move again, bloody hell I'm f**king skint, I'm staying here - it's my forever home.

"I will have my kids in this home I bloody love it. It's now all been changed to how I want it."

A spokesman for Northumbria Police said: "We can confirm we received a report of an intruder on a property in Gosforth.

"We have spoken to the occupant of the address to offer them reassurance and advice.

"Enquiries are ongoing to establish the identity of the intruder and whether any criminal offences have taken place."

The starlet first splashed out on the five-bedroom gaffe which boasts 0.28 acres of land and is located on the outskirts of Newcastle earlier this year, following her split with her co-star ex Sam Gowland.
