I Put TikTok's Viral Hair Saver To The Test

Publish date: 2024-09-04

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

If you struggle with damaged, frizzy hair, TikTok has the perfect shampoo and conditioner for you. &Honey’s Deep Moist Shampoo has been all the rage on TikTok, where fans rave that it gave new life to their damaged hair, leaving their locks feeling moisturized and brand new. &Honey’s Deep Moist conditioner is just as popular, and fans say the Japanese brand has a product for every hair type—no matter what your concern is.

As someone who has struggled with damaged hair after constant bleaching and dyeing, I decided to give into the hype and try &Honey’s Deep Moist Shampoo myself. The results? My damaged hair felt refreshed and hydrated after just one wash. After years of compromising the health of my hair with bleach, the promise of shiny soft hair seemed years away. I was shocked the TikTok-viral shampoo gave me results so soon. 

I was skeptical before using &Honey’s Deep Moist Shampoo. Moisturizing shampoos I’ve used in the past haven’t always answered my hair concerns, but instead left my hair greasy and heavy. Not to mention, living in New York City’s humid climate means I also have oily hair to worry about. My ideal shampoo would clean my oily scalp and rehydrate my brittle ends—a combination that’s hard to find.

Thankfully, the &Honey Deep Moist Shampoo succeeded at both. Right off the bat, it smelled amazing and lathered beautifully, which made me feel like I was washing away all of the oil my scalp was building up. I worked the shampoo throughout my hair, in order to moisturize my dry ends. After conditioning and letting my hair air dry, I immediately noticed a difference. My hair was soft to the touch and looked shiny in the light, without feeling overly greasy or heavy. (Honestly, I was surprised my hair could look so healthy.) Not to mention, the sweet scent lingered and left me feeling like I’d just come home from an expensive salon.

While &Honey’s Deep Moist Shampoo is my go-to, the brand has tons of other TikTok-viral products that are great for different hair types. &Honey’s Melty range is for frizzy hair and boosts your hair’s glow. While the brand’s Herb Smooth range softens your hair with organic herbal ingredients. If you struggle with fine and tangly hair, &Honey’s Silky range is for you. And for all of the cherry blossom fans, &Honey also has a whole cherry-blossom-scented range to hydrate your hair. 

If you’re like me and struggle with dry and damaged hair, the &Honey Deep Moist range is your best bet. Deep Moist products moisturize your hair leaving it silky smooth. In addition to the shampoo, the range also includes a conditioner (or treatment ) and a hair oil . All of which claim to repair your hair without harsh chemicals. Conveniently, the Deep Moist range works on all hair textures, whether your hair’s straight, wavy, curly, or somewhere in between.
