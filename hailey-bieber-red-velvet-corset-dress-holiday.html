Hailey Bieber Is So Festive in This Red Velvet Corset Dress

Publish date: 2024-09-20

Hailey Bieber has the perfect outfit to be the best-dressed guest at any holiday party.

The model dressed up to kick off her 27th birthday celebration earlier this week. She got in the holiday spirit with her fit, a strapless velvet corset midi dress in a deep red. The cupped bra curled up at the sides, and the fitted bodice hugged Bieber’s torso, creating a flattering hourglass silhouette.

Bieber accessorized simply with gold hoop earrings and a gold watch. She had her hair up in an elegant updo, with strands of hair framing her face, and opted for glowy, rosy makeup for the evening, including shimmery shadow and a glossy pink lip. Her pointy burgundy manicure perfectly matched her dress.

“Starting the bday week off early 🙆🏼‍♀️,” she wrote in an Instagram post featuring photos of her look. The Rhode founder turns 27 on November 22.

Bieber recently wore another strapless red dress—albeit a summer edition—to celebrate the launch of her strawberry lip peptide treatment. The piece was from Ermanno Scervino’s Fall 2023 collection, which Bieber adorably styled with strawberry-shaped earrings and her big (for Bieber) silver necklace. She finished off the party look with a red leather handbag, elongated brown rectangular sunglasses, and vintage-inspired red peep-toe heels.

“I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself,” she told Harper’s Bazaar last year of her approach to dressing. “Even if I’m just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!”
