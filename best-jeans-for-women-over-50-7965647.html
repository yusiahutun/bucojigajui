The 17 Best Jeans for Women Over 50 in 2024

Publish date: 2024-05-22

No matter how old you get, denim is a fashion staple that is timeless. For women over 50, although their relationship with fashion evolves, jeans remain a reliable choice. Unlike the younger generations, whose tastes often are influenced by social media and what celebrities are wearing, the more experienced generation favor classic cuts and more sophisticated styles.

“Most women, once they hit 50, gain extra weight around the middle during menopause so there are several things to think about their newer bodies,” says stylist Michelle Barrett. “Look for a medium waist with a straight leg. A medium waist will cover that lower tummy area many women are not comfortable with.” 

There are of course other flattering silhouettes such as high-waisted or bootcut jeans. We spoke to celebrity stylists, fashion experts and tested out pairs ourselves to find the best jeans for women over 50. We looked for wide size ranges, comfortable yet supportive materials, and silhouettes and washes that make you feel confident at any age.

Best Overall

Amazon

What We Love: There’s standard, petite, and plus-size options available.

What We Don’t Love: The sizing can be inconsistent between washes.

Look no further for the ultimate pair of jeans — Gloria Vanderbilt has designed this collection with a range of women in mind. This Amazon favorite boasts an impressive 78,000 reviews, catering to every woman's needs, be it standard, petite, or plus size. This classic high-rise taper jean embodies comfort and style, complete with functional pockets, an easy zipper fly, and over 60 different washes to pick from. In fact, Priyanka Chopra says she can spend all day in these jeans and feel comfortable. Plus, a pair is only $12, so at this price range can you really only buy just one?

Hitting right at your natural waistline, these jeans don’t cut into your middle and extra support helps smooth your middle. Not too high waisted or too low, versatility reigns supreme as these jeans effortlessly pair with a spectrum of tops, from chic fashion pieces to casual tees, and look good with various footwear choices. Due to all the different washes, some reviewers say the sizing is inconsistent between colors, so you may want to order a couple options and use Amazon’s great return policy.

What to Keep in Mind

Silhouette

Though you can wear any jean silhouette at any age, our experts share those that they find best for those over the age of 50. “Straight-fit jeans are a great choice for people of all ages and sizes and are a timeless option you’ll want to wear for years to come,” says fashion stylist Alicia Lloyd. “For women in their 50s, I also recommend a bootcut or slightly flared denim for a versatile and chic look.” We like Levi's Women's 315 Shaping Bootcut Jean for a classic bootcut jean.

Wash

Similar to the silhouette, you want to think about the wash of the jean. “A classic blue jean is timeless; lighter blue is beautiful for spring-summer and darker blue going to fall-winter,” says fashion stylist Catherine Bibeau. “A nice midnight black is chic as well for a dinner or work outing.” The Daria Jean is our go-to black jean that's formal enough for the office and events.

One thing to keep in mind is taking care of your jeans so that the wash doesn’t fade. Fashion stylist Liz Wendler recommends washing jeans before wearing to help remove any dye left from production to avoid staining anything white, like your sneakers or couch. Be sure to turn them inside out and wash cold and then hang dry them until they are fully dry. After which, put them in the dryer for no more than 10 minutes to soften the stiffness of the denim and avoid washing after every wear.

Fit

Although many brands like Measure & Made and Good American are actively trying to fix challenges in achieving uniform sizing and accessibility, there is still more work to be done. As a result, put aside the label and trust your instincts. When shopping for the best jeans, request various sizes within your range to find the perfect length for your height and torso. There are options made for short torsos as well as long inseams and vice versa. Instead of fixating on the number of the size, rely on the mirror and your ability to style to guide your decision-making. You can even find the perfect jeans to make a flat butt look perkier.

Your Questions, Answered

How do you style jeans as a woman over 50?

Styling jeans is all about harmonizing comfort and sophistication, according to Wendler.

“Opt for well-fitting, classic cuts like high-waisted or straight-leg designs. Pair them with a tailored blouse, a chic blazer, or a cozy sweater for timeless elegance,” says Wendler. “Accessorize with statement jewelry and a stylish belt to elevate the look. Embrace versatile footwear such as ankle boots, loafers, or classic pumps.”

Don’t also be afraid to experiment to find your style, according to fashion stylist Ashley Full. “If you want to jazz it up, a subtle sequin top to pair back to your denim wash cuffed jeans,” says Full. “Tone her down with a great sneaker and you will look on trend and chic.”

Can you wear skinny or ripped jeans over 50?

While many associate skinny and ripped jeans with their teenage years, that doesn’t mean you can’t wear them past a certain age. At InStyle, we believe you can wear anything that makes you confident and happy, so skinny jeans are absolutely doable! In fact, Bibeau recommends a classic skinny jean, like Measure & Made Skinny Ankle Jean, for women over their 50s who want to show off their figure. And as for ripped jeans, there are plenty of styles with distressed details that work for all ages. Jen7 Slim Boyfriend Jeans has a small rip that adds to the casual feel of the pant.
