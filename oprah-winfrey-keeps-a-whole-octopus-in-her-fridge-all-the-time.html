Oprah Winfrey keeps 'a whole octopus in her fridge' all the time

Publish date: 2024-03-09

Have you ever wondered what billionaires keep in their fridge? Well Oprah Winfrey has finally told us, and it’s an octopus – yep.

If you were playing general trivia with your friends about your favorite celebrity, you may not have got this question right, sorry. But Oprah Winfrey revealed she keeps a ‘whole octopus’ in her fridge all the time. Perhaps we don’t follow the same routine as the billionaire TV star, but it seems to make her partner happy.

Oprah Winfrey keeps an octopus in the fridge for her partner

Speaking on Jimmy Kimmel Live! over her new primetime special about weight loss drugs, Oprah Winfrey was talking to the host about her own weight journey.

She previously admitted to taking Ozempic, the star-favorite drug to combat weight gain and help shed pounds.

Now she’s releasing a special where she’s talking to people who have taken the ‘miracle’ drug, Wegovy as well as experts on the subject.

But one strange detail came out in the interview, and it’s how Oprah Winfrey keeps a “whole octopus in the fridge on a regular basis”.

Huh?

She explained: “Stedman loves octopus,” referencing her longtime partner Stedman Graham who is 73.

The TV star says he eats octopus for breakfast “almost every day” but she thinks it’s a “strange thing” too.

But Oprah Winfrey added about her fridge and his eating habits: “You know what it is, it’s not just octopus, it’s octopus and okra”.

The rumors about Oprah Winfrey and her dogs

Jimmy also wanted to ask her about various rumors that do the rounds on the internet, strange theories, that emerge about the star.

He said: “There is a rumor, it has been published that your dogs are set to inherit $30 million when you pass away. Is that true?

“That is not true,” Oprah replied. “First of al, unfortunately, most of my dogs have also passed away.

“Did they leave you any money?” Jimmy jokes.

“Didn’t leave me a dime,” Oprah quips. “They left me with medical bills is what they left me. No”.

Aside from that, she did confirm she was going to be buried under the oak treat at her mansion in Montecito. But the tree fell so she’s scrapped that idea. The TV star also explained it was true she tried to buy the Los Angeles Clippers basketball team alongside music mogul David Geffen when they were for sale. But it didn’t happen.

Oprah Winfrey opened up over her personal weight loss journey, but it didn’t sound like she ate the octopus sitting in the fridge every day to do it.

She praised exercise, walking every day, and fasting – not eating after 4pm – and watching what she ate to her 40lb weight loss.
