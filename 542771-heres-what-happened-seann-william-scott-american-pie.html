What happened to Seann William Scott after American Pie?

After landing the role of Steve Stifler in American Pie, he reprised it in three different films from the same franchise, namely:

  • American Pie 2 in 2001
  • American Wedding in 2003
  • American Reunion in 2012

According to The Looper, his role as Steve Stifler made him a victim of typecasting. He became the casting director's go-to person for all their high school party boy, college jock, or aimless postgraduates despite being way past the age of 20s.

Even though typecasting earned him numerous film and TV roles, he eventually fell into the casting trap. His audience became so associated with these roles that it was hard for them to see him as anything else. As a result, this limited the roles he was allowed to play or offered.
