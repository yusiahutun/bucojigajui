Jennifer Lopez Rides Around Miami in a Sports Bra and Leggings

Publish date: 2024-03-14
  • Jennifer Lopez was photographed riding around Miami on an ElliptiGO trainer.
  • The 50-year-old star wore leggings by Niyama Sol, which feature renderings of different phases of the moon; a black sports bra; and a tie-dye mask.

Jennifer Lopez is making moves in more ways than one. Not only did she hint at making music with Colombian superstar Maluma (posting a pic of them together in the studio yesterday), she is clearly keeping her famed physique in tip-top shape.

The 50-year-old star was photographed riding around Miami on a ElliptiGO, which is a cross between a bicycle, scooter, and elliptical (yes, we had to Google what it was). Indeed, over the course of the pandemic and while self-quarantining in the home she shares with fiancé Alex Rodriguez, J.Lo has been trying out a number of fitness routines—from traditional weights to playing baseball to now riding around on this nifty contraption. And through all of it, she has kept her style on point.

Niyama Sol Moon Child High Waisted Legging

Niyama Sol Moon Child High Waisted Legging

This time, Lopez wore high-waisted leggings by Niyama Sol, featuring renderings of different phases of the moon and a starry night. The choice bottoms were certainly apropos for anyone with a body like hers—you know, someone who has legs and a derriere that are truly out of this world. Still, it is clear that she is in touch with reality: Throughout her ride, J.Lo made sure that her tie-dye mask was covering her face at all times. Because safety first, people!
