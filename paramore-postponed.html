Paramore Postponed

Publish date: 2024-09-06

San Francisco, CA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Paramore regret to announce that all dates beginning in San Francisco on October 4 through October 9th in St. Paul will be postponed due to a case of laryngitis for singer Hayley Williams. The tour will resume in Chicago on October 10th. Tickets for all postponed dates will be honored at the rescheduled dates listed below. Refunds may also be claimed at point of purchase.
