How guys text when they like you: 10 signs he is not just being nice

Publish date: 2024-01-27

For centuries, girls and boys have used body language to determine whether they like each other. But in a modern era where text messaging is common, knowing how guys text when they like you can be confusing. Generally, text messaging indicates desire, romance, and interest. However, since you cannot read body language, how often a guy texts you can give you a clear perspective of the relationship you have with him, irrespective of the stage of the relationship.

Today, a common notion is that you will know whether a guy likes you depending on how often he texts you. But a guy can text you daily and not even be interested in you. For example, he texts you every day to talk about sex or constantly responds with memes.

Conversely, he may take a long time to text you, but the messages show he is more interested in you. Unless you know how guys text when they like you, it may be challenging to get an answer to their intentions.

Would a player text you every day?

It is never easy to know a player’s intentions. If he is trying to manipulate you emotionally, he will do anything to make you believe he is in love with you. But, of course, that also means he will text you every day or occasionally.

One thing to understand is that even if a guy texts you daily or occasionally, you can be confident that he is interested in something you have to offer. Therefore, you have to move fast to understand his intentions. Otherwise, he could be playing you.

How often do guys text if they like you?

Guys are different. Some text you a lot. Others take their time. But when a guy texts you often, it means he is more interested in you. However, the frequency of his text messages does not mean anything.

A guy may text you a lot because he is playing you and trying to manipulate you emotionally. Also, he can text you frequently because he is desperate. In other cases, he will only text you occasionally, probably after two or three days. If you want to know whether he likes you, look at the content of his text messages.

How guys text when they like you

The content of a guy’s text can speak a lot and help you determine whether he likes you or not. If you want to be sure about his intentions, here are ways guys hint they like you through text.

1. He asks questions about your life

When a guy likes you, he will ask you many questions about you or your life. For example, he will want to know more about your childhood, hopes and dreams, and what makes you happiest.

The guy will ask you about your family, likes and dislikes, favourite colour, your job, troubles, or even want to know how you fared during the day. An interested guy will also avoid asking you creepy questions.

2. He replies quickly

A quick reply from a guy during texting offers a sure way of knowing whether he is interested in talking to you. He has responded fast because he likes you and wants to hear what you say. Furthermore, the quick reply could suggest he is open and honest because he did not think about it.

3. He compliments you all the time

One tell-tale sign that a man likes you is that he looks for any opportunity to compliment you. Therefore, he will take his time on the phone trying to find the best words to compliment you.

A compliment about your appearance indicates that he is into you. Also, when he emotionally compliments you via text, praising your kindness, devotion, and sincerity, he tells you he likes you.

4. He replies with the heart emoji

Guys use the heart emoji to express romance. If he sends you the throbbing heart emoji, it means he likes you and is into you. Furthermore, a red heart emoji is shared in a warm emotional context. When you receive it from a guy, he expresses his gratitude, love, or happiness. The heart could also mean he is being flirtatious.

5. He texts you first

When a guy texts you first, whether in the morning or randomly, it could mean a lot of things. The most obvious reason is that he enjoys talking to you. But a person cannot enjoy talking to you if he does not like you. Therefore, getting a text from him first indicates that he likes you. It can also signify that he is thinking about you.

6. He writes you long texts

While it may not often happen, a guy who likes you will be comfortable writing you a long text message. He may even break it into several sections to put his message across. However, the content of the text message is crucial to decipher. If he sends you a long sweet message, it implies he likes you.

7. He does not send multiple texts simultaneously

Double texting can be confusing to decipher. In some cases, it could mean a guy is interested in you. However, a shy guy texting habit is to avoid sending you multiple texts because it makes him look desperate.

If he likes you, he will give you time to respond. But if he sends you many texts simultaneously, it implies he is clingy and possessive. Also, it could mean he wants to control the flow of the conversation.

8. He personalises his texts

One way in which a guy can personalise his texts is by including your name. Generally, when a guy includes your name in a text, it creates a feeling of intimacy. Also, the personalisation draws you closer to him.

When you read your name in the message, you feel understood, respected, and acknowledged. Another reason is that he wants to get your attention through text.

9. He texts you randomly

Perhaps the best way to know whether a guy likes you is when he texts you randomly. Of course, a random text can mean different things depending on the relationship. However, it could mean he misses you. The guy may also send you the text in the middle of the day if he wants to check up on you and determine how things are going on with you.

10. He never gives excuses

People often give excuses to justify a fault or an offence. Its primary goal is to lessen responsibility and get you to overlook what he did or even forgive him. But when a guy never gives you excuses, it means he likes you and has taken responsibility for what has happened. So, for example, he will not give excuses for not replying to your texts even though he knows he was busy at work.

Unless you understand or know how guys text when they like you, it will be challenging for you to determine their intentions. However, you will know he likes you if he asks questions about your life, replies quickly to your texts, complements you all the time, texts you randomly, personalises his texts, or replies with a heart emoji. But if he only talks about sex or responds with memes, he is not interested in you and could be playing you.
