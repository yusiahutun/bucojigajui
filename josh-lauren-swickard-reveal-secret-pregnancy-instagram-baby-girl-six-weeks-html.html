Josh and Lauren Swickard reveal secret pregnancy on Instagram with baby girl due in about six weeks

Publish date: 2024-04-13

Josh and Lauren Swickard took to Instagram on Tuesday to reveal they have a baby girl on the way.

Lauren, 27, posted photos on Instagram of her baby bump after she and Josh, 28, kept it under wraps for months.

'@joshswickard and I have kept this secret long enough. Our dream come true. Baby girl, we can't wait to meet you! 6 weeks until you're here!!! We love you SO MUCH! #33weekspregnant #secretsout #babygirl,' Lauren wrote in the caption for her roughly 106,000 followers.
