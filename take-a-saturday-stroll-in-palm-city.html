Take a Saturday Stroll in Palm City

Publish date: 2024-02-06

Enjoy the great outdoors and preview the highly anticipated Kiplinger Conservancy’s KC Trails, a new public trail system within Mattamy Homes’ forthcoming Newfield master planned community in Palm City, during Saturday Strolls on March 23 and 30.

Mattamy recently launched Saturday Strolls earlier this month, allowing visitors to enjoy a sneak peek of the work on the site’s natural resources. Guests can capture photos and videos of the future gopher tortoise preserve, as well as the area’s wildlife, birds, flowers, and more.

Saturday Strolls are from 9 a.m. to noon. Register for the March 23 and March 30 events here and here. The series will continue next month, with Saturday Strolls already scheduled for April 6 and 13.
