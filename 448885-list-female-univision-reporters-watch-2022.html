List of all female Univision reporters to watch in 2022

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Univision is one of the most-watched channels for Spanish language content. Viewers from across the United States tune in to the station to have a glimpse of what is happening around the world. Part of its success comes from its female Univision reporters, who are exceptional at news reporting and presentation.

Univision is a diversified Spanish-language media company with interests in television, radio and the internet. The company was founded in the early 1960s, and it has risen through the ranks to become the most-watched Spanish-language television network in the United States. The company headquarters are in Miami.

List of all female Univision reporters

If you love watching the news, then you will agree that it has a lot to do with the presentation too. This is something that the Univision newscast has mastered. Here is a list of the female Univision reporters you need to watch in 2022.

1. Ilia Calderon

  • Full names: Ilia Calderon Chamat
  • Date of birth: May 15, 1972
  • Age: 50 years old as of 2022
  • Place of birth: Choco, Colombia
  • Profession: Journalist

Ilia is one of the most famous female news anchors on Univision. She is currently serving as the news anchor for the national evening newscast show on the channel alongside Jorge Ramos. Before this role, she worked at Telemundo, where she served as the host of the Noticiero Telemundo weekend edition.

She is the first Afro-Latina to anchor a weekday primetime newscast for a major broadcast station in the US. Ilia is a holder of a social work degree from Universidad de Antioquia.

  • Full names: Angela Patricia Janiot Martirena

  • Age: 59 years old as of 2022
  • Place of birth: Santander, Colombia

Patricia is a famous Colombian journalist and news anchor. She is currently serving as the news anchor and senior correspondent for Univision. Before this role, she was the senior news anchor for CNN en Espanol, a position she held for 25 years.

She is also a former model. Patricia participated in various pageants, including Miss Colombia (1983) and Miss World (1984). She is an alumnus of Universidad de la Sabana in Bogota, where she graduated with a degree in journalism.

3. Maity Interiano

  • Full names: Maria Teresa Interiano Medina
  • Date of birth: January 15, 1985
  • Age: 37 years old as of 2022
  • Place of birth: Gainesville, Florida
  • Profession: Journalist, anchor, and television producer

Maity is one of the lead reporters and correspondents at Univision. She is currently serving as the co-anchor of Noticiero Univision Fin de Semanas alongside Felix de Bedout. Maity is also part of the Despierta America morning show.

  • Date of birth: August 16, 1981

5. Teresa Rodriguez

  • Full names: Teresa Rodriguez
  • Date of birth: February 26, 1962

Teresa is one of the most popular female Spanish news anchors. She is renowned for her current role as the co-anchor of Aqui y Ahora, a primetime award-winning news magazine program airing on Univision.

In addition, she hosts the weekly segments titled Primer Plano and America through Spanish Eyes. Before joining Univision, she served as the anchor of WTVJ-Channel 4. She holds a bachelor's degree in economics and marketing from Barry University.

  • Date of birth: September 5, 1967

Ana has also been part of the cast of the Un Nuevo Dia morning show on Telemundo.

10. Satcha Pretto

  • Full names: Satcha Pretto
  • Date of birth: April 5, 1980

She has worked for several other media companies, including KTLE-Telemundo, KWES-TV, and KUVN Channel 23. Satcha graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communications from Angelo State University in Texas.

She has been honoured with the Lifetime Achievement Award thanks to her outstanding performance as the show's host. Besides hosting the show, she is recognized for her work as one of the panel judges on Mira Quien Baila.
