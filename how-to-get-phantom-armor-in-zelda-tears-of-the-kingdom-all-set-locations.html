How to get Phantom Armor in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Publish date: 2024-02-27

In this Phantom Armor Tears of the Kingdom guide, we’ll cover how to find all three separate pieces of the TOTK armour set.

This is a very cool set based on the Phantom guardians from The Legend of Zelda: Spirit Tracks on the DS. What’s more, it gives you a boost to your defence and attack when equipped.

Keep reading to find out where you can get all three pieces of this set in the game, so keep reading

Where to find the Phantom Helmet in Tears of the Kingdom

The helmet part of the Phantom Armor set in Tears of the Kingdom can be found in a cave just north of Puffer Beach. This is a beach along the southern coast of Hyrule near the Highland Stable and the Lake of the Horse God.

There’s a stretch of beach where you can find some Zonai rockets right next to a pool. Take one and fuse it to your shield, then use it to blast upwards through the Luminous Stone deposits. This will take you up into Puffer Beach Overhead Cave.

Defeat the Horriblins in here then head to the back of the cave. The Phantom Helmet will be waiting for you.

Where to find the Phantom Chest Armor in Tears of the Kingdom

You can find the Phantom Armor chest piece in the Tamio River Downstream Cave, which is positioned near the Gerudo Highlands Skyview Tower.

Enter the cave in the location marked on the map above. Defeat all the enemies inside, then continue through the cave. At the forked path, you can head right to find a Bubbulfrog, then head left to continue towards the treasure.

You’ll enter into a large cavern with Gloom Hands down below. You can either make your way to the treasure by climbing (this will take a lot of stamina) or you can go down to the left of it and go up the path. There are rocks you can break here, so destroy them and continue on to get to the Phantom Armor chest piece.

Where to find the Phantom Greaves in Tears of the Kingdom

This final part of the Phantom Armor set in Tears of the Kingdom can be found in Gerudo Desert. Travel to the Gerudo Canyon Skyview Tower then head to the East Barrens.

There are lots of holes in the sand here. You’ll have to fall through the correct hole in the sand to get to the right location. If you choose the wrong hole you’ll end up in a different part of the underground ruins. There’s also a statue down here that will point to the location of the greaves.

Go through the hole marked on the map above and descend to the treasure. You will now have the Phantom Greaves and all three parts of the Phantom Armor set in Tears of the Kingdom!
