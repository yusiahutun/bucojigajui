Who is Tieghan Gerard?

Tieghan Gerard is a famous, talented American food photographer and blogger, recipe developer, stylist, cookbook author, and self-taught cook. She was born on 15th September 1993 in Ohio, United States.

Tieghan is the only girl in a family of ten. Her parents are Jen Gerard and Conrad Gerard, and she has seven brothers: Creighton, Trevor, Malachi, Brendan, Red, Asher, and Oslo.

Making a "big" meal is essential in a big family like hers. To make dinnertime less chaotic and ensure everyone gets fed on time, Tieghan started helping with cooking. It was a bit crazy at first, but she grew to love it and eventually took charge of preparing all their meals.

She documented the cooking process with photos and kept track of her best recipes, which led to the creation of the Half Baked Harvest blog. Tieghan lived in Cleveland, Ohio, for the first 14 years of her life before her family moved to the snowy mountains of Colorado.

Tieghan Gerard career

Tieghan Gerard started her Half Baked Harvest food blog in 2012. It quickly became famous because of her wholesome recipes with natural and healthy ingredients. She has authored several cookbooks, including Overnight, Easy Comfort Foods, Meal-Prepped, and Half Baked Harvest Super Simple: More Than 125 Recipes for Instant.

They have since appeared on popular platforms like Food Network, Shape Magazine, and The Cooking Channel. In both 2014 and 2016, Half Baked Harvest was chosen as the reader's favourite food blog by Better Homes and Gardens.

In 2016, it received the prestigious Bloglovin' Best Food Blog Award and earned Saveur Magazine's Award for the most creative weeknight dinners.
