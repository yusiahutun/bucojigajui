The ShopBAZAAR Hero: Tibi Handbag Exclusive

Publish date: 2024-07-02

Every week on ShopBAZAAR.com, we feature one Hero—an item that deserves your keenest attention—carefully chosen by a Harper's Bazaar editor. This week it is Tibi's debut handbag. Crafted in a luxurious and modern navy leather, with a sportif accent stripe, it's the accessory to have and to hold from one of our favorite American brands.

"Created in collaboration with Myriam Schaefer, and produced in Italy, the bags are equal parts effortlessly chic and instant classic."

Tibi by Myriam Schaefer bag, $895 (exclusive), shopBAZAAR.com.
