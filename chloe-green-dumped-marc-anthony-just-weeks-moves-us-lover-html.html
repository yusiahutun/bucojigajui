Jennifer Lopez's ex Marc Anthony has dumped Chloe Green just weeks after the Topshop heiress moved to the US, according to claims.

Father-of-five Marc reportedly told Chloe their romance was over and the designer, who at 22 is just three years older than her lover's oldest daughter, moved her belongings out of their shared Miami house.

Chloe, whose father Sir Philip is worth an estimated £3.8billion, was first spotted out with J-Lo's ex in February.

'It seems to have been Marc's decision': Meanwhile, the I Need to Know hitmaker has been single ever since dumping Topshop heiress Chloe Green last April after a two-month romance

It is thought Sir Philip, who is good friends with Marc. had given his seal of approval to the union.

A source told The Sun On Sunday: 'The pair got serious very quickly and had the blessing of her parents, but it hasn't worked out.

'It seems to have been Marc's decision to end it.'

The pal added the decision to split was sudden and Chloe has now left Miami to go on holiday with friends to recover.

Hoping for third-time lucky? Twice-married Marc looked thrilled to attend Amber Ridinger and Duane Mclaughlin at Dorado Beach earlier this year

Marc was married to J-Lo for eight years and has twins Emme Maribel Muñiz and Maximillian David Muñiz with the star.

He also has a daughter Ariana from his relationship with former New York police officer Debbie Rosado and two sons with ex-wife former Miss Universe Dayanara Torres.

Sir Philip holidayed with Marc and J-Lo in 2010.

Chloe's exes include Djibril Cisse and Jermain Defoe.

Earlier in the relationship the pair were believed to have been smitten, with the UK based Chloe moving over to the US for her lover.

Married: Marc and J-Lo were married for six years and had two children, they are said to remain friends since the split

Seal of approval: Chloe's father Philip, who is worth an estimated £3.8million, was said to have given the union his blessing

A source told Grazia magazine: 'Chloe and Marc have totally fallen for one another. She's been looking into getting a place in New York, where they love to hang out, or Miami, where his family are based.'

According to heat magazine, Sir Philip flew out to Miami to spend the Easter weekend with Chloe and Marc, as well as her mother Tina and brother Brandon, where the group got some dutch courage for the family meeting by drinking Dom Perignon at local club Set.

MailOnline has contacted Chloe and Marc for a comment.
