Here's a calendar that makes scent prepping your home for the holidays a no-brainer. Not only does it feature 12 of Yankee Candle's seasonal scents, including Balsam and Cedar, Christmas Cookie, and Sparkling Snow, but the Advent also nicely features a decorative tea light candle holder.

After lighting the cedar- and balsam-noted Noble Fir and Pine candle, vanilla bean- and peppermint-scented Candy Cane and Cream, among others, it's easy to get lost in the holiday spirit.
