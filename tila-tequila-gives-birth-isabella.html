Tila Tequila Gives Birth to Daughter Isabella Monroe

Publish date: 2024-05-28

Tila Tequila is a mom.

The former MTV reality star welcomed her first child into the world on Sunday, November 17 ... a daughter named Isabella Monroe Nguyen.

Tequila -- real name Tila Nguyen -- confirms the news to toofab, telling us "She's soooo perfect and beautiful! I am completely in love with her."

The baby weighed in at 7 pounds, 2 ounces and is 20 inches long.

The 33-year-old knew this baby was coming, predicting her labor in a blog post marking her 40th week of pregnancy that went up on Saturday afternoon.

"I have a feeling that soon after this blog goes up.... this crazy baby will finally make her grand entrance into this world," she wrote at the time. "I know for a fact that I am really going to miss being pregnant because to be honest, I have really, really enjoyed my pregnancy so far! I love being able to feel my baby move inside of me, I know when she's happy, I know she hears me when I talk to her. I just feel this incredible bond with my daughter and I feel like she is so safe in there and protected from all harm! Maybe that's why she doesn't wanna come out?"

Tila has not revealed who the father of her child is and says she will be "raising her alone."

"My daughter will grow up to be a leader and stand up for all of the people who cannot stand up for themselves. My daughter will be beautiful just like her mama, but at the same time I will raise her to be a humble and loving person who will respect herself and others," she said in an earlier blog post. "I know I'm going to be a fantastic Mother to my little Warrior Princess Baby Girl because she will see how much crap that her mommy has been through in life."
