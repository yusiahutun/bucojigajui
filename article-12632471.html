Aldi issues urgent recall for teriyaki beef stir fry because it contains wheat

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Aldi has issued an urgent recall after wheat was found inside a popular ready meal.

The retailer has asked customers to check if they have purchased its £4 Ready Set Cook Teriyaki Beef Stir Fry.

The supermarket has warned the product contains wheat (gluten) which is not listed on the label. 

This undeclared ingredient poses a potential health risk for individuals with coeliac disease, or those with an allergy or intolerance to wheat or gluten.

The Food Standards Agency has released a statement containing information about the affected product brand and the updated use-by date.

The UK's cheapest supermarket, Aldi, has issued an urgent recall after wheat (gluten) was found inside a popular ready meal 

The FSA said: 'Aldi GB is recalling the above product from customers and has contacted the relevant allergy support organisations, which will tell their members about the recall. 

The company has also issued a recall notice to its customers. These notices explain to customers why the product is being recalled and tell them what to do if they have bought the product.'

This notice affects all dates up to and including 22 October 2023, for the 500g packs of Ready Set Cook Teriyaki Beef Stir Fry.

The retailer has asked customers to check if they have purchased its £4 Ready Set Cook Teriyaki Beef Stir Fry

If you have purchased this product and have coeliac disease, or an allergy or intolerance to wheat or gluten, do not eat it.

Instead, return it to the store where it was bought to receive a full refund.

Aldi said: 'As a precautionary measure, our supplier is recalling this product because it is incorrectly labelled and contains wheat (gluten).

'Customers are asked to return this product to their nearest store, where a full refund will be given.'

Shoppers have been told they can visit help.aldi.co.uk or call customer services on 0800 042 0800 for further information.
