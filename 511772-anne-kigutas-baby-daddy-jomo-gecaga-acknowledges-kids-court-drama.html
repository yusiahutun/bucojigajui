Rich kid of Kenya Jomo Gecaga has publicly acknowledged his children in a sweet post

Jomo and his baby mamas

Jomo, who is former president Uhuru Kenyatta's nephew, has three baby mamas namely Valentine Njoroge, Anne Kiguta, and Lola Hannigan.

Word on the street has it that the former Citizen TV presenter is allegedly suing Jomo for KSh 415 million child support or KSh 2.7 million per month upkeep for allegedly neglecting their children.

On June 18, during Father's Day, Lola celebrated Jomo with a sweet message, describing him as an incredible dad.

She shared an adorable video of her baby daddy strolling with their daughter, Malaika.

"Happy Father’s Day, my love. You are such an incredible dad. ," she captioned the video.

Commenting on Lola's post, Gecaga acknowledged all his five children, and he said:

"My Malaika, in name and in spirit. May you always watch over us and your siblings. @lolahannigan thank you, but it takes two. Udi, Uhuru, Makena, Malaika and X, thank you for entrusting me to be your dad ❤️."
