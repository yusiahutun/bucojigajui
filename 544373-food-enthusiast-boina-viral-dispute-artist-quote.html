Food Enthusiast Boina Goes Viral Over Dispute with Make-Up Artist: &quot;Aongezee Pesa&quot;

Publish date: 2024-07-28
  • Boina went viral after she debuted on the TikTok app as a food enthusiast, and netizens were amused by her exceptionally wild appetite
  • The young lady shares videos showing what she eats in a day, including meals and numerous snacks
  • Make-up artist, Veejes Creative and the food enthusiast were embroiled in an online feud, and Boina trended on the X app

Food enthusiast Boina has gone viral over a collaboration dispute with make-up artist Veejes Creative.

Why is Boina trending?

The make-up artist (MUA) had reached out to the TikToker for a possible collaboration, and she agreed to their agreement.

According to the MUA, Veejes Boina showed up for the shoot, and she later took several videos and pictures.

They had agreed to share the content, and a week later, the make-up artist shared a meme with Boina as the cover photo.

The food enthusiast called Veejes, asking why she was tarnishing her name. Boina then changed her mind about appearing on the MUA's Instagram page.

"She called me and told me she doesn't want us to post. I asked her the reason, and she said that it does not align with her content. She asked how much is the makeup I told her and she said that she would pay. I told her that even if she paid, I would still post it because it was not enough for the hours used..."

The MUA shared videos of Boina on her Instagram page, and she threatened to take legal action against her.

Vejees noted that for her to take down the video, the TikToker should make additional payments for her time.

"If she wants me to take down the video, she should add some cash to compromise the work I lost and also my work as a photographer, videographer, and editor."

The TikToker has since deleted the video she shared on her Instagram account.

How did Boina go viral?

In 2023, Boina steadily gained followers courtesy of her exceptionally healthy appetite.

The young lady shared videos showing what she ate in a day, including meals and numerous snacks.

Kenyans on TikTok were astounded by her appetite, with some comparing it to that of construction workers.
