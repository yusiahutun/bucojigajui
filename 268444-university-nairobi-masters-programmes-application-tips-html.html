University of Nairobi requirements for a masters programme

Applicants for masters degree programmes should have the following:

  • A minimum of upper second class honours degree in the relevant discipline from the University of Nairobi or equivalent qualification from institutions recognized by senate.
  • Persons with lower qualifications but with postgraduate diplomas after Bachelors' degrees are also considered for admission.
  • KCSE/O-Level certificate.
  • Certified copies of undergraduate degree certificate and transcripts.
  • Applicants with KCSE C (plain) to attach certified copy of diploma from KNEC
  • Applicants with KCSE C- (minus) to attach certified copies of a certificate and a diploma.

University of Nairobi online application process

The University of Nairobi uses an online application system for both undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Follow the steps below to make your application.

  • Visit the University of Nairobi's online application portal.
  • If you are applying for the first time, create a personal profile by clicking on "Register".
  • Proceed to fill out all the required information. All details marked with a red asterisk are mandatory.
  • An email will be sent to you. Log in into your email account and activate your account together with your username and password.
  • Once activated log in to your account using your registered email and password and proceed with the application process.
  • Complete all the six steps from primary information to programme application.
  • Click "Next" to head to the payment page.
  • Pay the required application fee. The system supports online payment using master and visa cards, M-Pesa and payment through the bank.
  • Once done submit your application.

    How long does masters take in the University of Nairobi?

    Typically, a master's degree program spans 1 to 2 years in duration. The curriculum is structured to cover a minimum of three and a maximum of ten semesters, with each semester lasting fifteen weeks.

    How much does University of Nairobi cost for international students?

    The University of Nairobi has a non-discriminatory fees policy. The tuition fees are the same for international students and Kenyan students. The exact cost will vary depending on your program of study.
