The Best Looks From the Coach 1941 Spring 2017 Show

Publish date: 2024-03-29

Fall is barely here, but in fashion, we're already thinking about spring, thanks to that sneak peek we get during New York Fashion Week. Which leaves us lusting for months until these pieces can finally be ours. Here are the best clothes and accessories from the latest Coach 1941 Collection that should graduate from the catwalk to your closet next season—sartorial superlatives, if you will.

1. Most Likely to Succeed: The Ankle Boot

The stylish statement-making shoe of the season comes platformed, studded, and fringed for spring—and gets along with just about everything in your closet, from sweet dresses to casual jeans.

2. Most Popular: The Army Parka

Look like the coolest one in the room without making a big deal of it. This army parka is a staple from mod culture in the '60s, updated to suit 21st-century sensibilities with sharp tailoring in a go-with-anything green.

3. Most Likely to Impress: The Ribbon Dress

You'll feel prim and proper yet somehow subversively grunge in this Victoriana high-neck, pouf-sleeve dress, complete with contrasting black ribbon ties up and down the front. It's delightfully old-fashioned, and definitely a "do."

4. Best Looking: Elvis Sweatshirt

Pasting Elvis' gorgeous mug all over short-sleeved sweatshirts for spring is one of the best things that has happened in fashion in ages. Casual, clever, and referential, this top gives even an ordinary pair of skinny black jeans the street cred they deserve.

5. Best Dress(ed): The Floral Midi

Once a hallmark of '90s grunge princesses everywhere, the floral midi has taken on a life of its own over the past quarter-century, and is now solidly cemented in our pop psyche as a new classic. Wear it to tea, the mall, or a rock show—this dress will be the most versatile piece in your wardrobe.

6. Most Outgoing: The Shark Sweater

A conversation starter for sure... This cheeky cozy knit is casual enough for those easy dressing days where you just want to get out the door—and charming enough for popping over a kicky little dress for dinner or a date.

7. Miss Congeniality: The Printed Moto Jacket

Simultaneously sweet and badass, this floral print motorcycle jacket works for day and night—uniting two classic fashion tropes in one perfect outerwear piece.

8. Most Likely to Be Famous: The Fringe Leather Dress

Drama queen, aspiring rockstar, or future fashion stylist—this over-the-top dress (with rivets!) can be worn on its own for day or night—or as a killer layering piece over skinny pants and under a bodysuit.

9. Most Photogenic: Fringed Suede Bag

This little shoulder bag inspired by cowboys and ranchers of the American West gets a revamp on its classic roots—its sleek boxy lines and chic metal chain are juxtaposed against a lush, generous fringe.

10. Valedictorian: Kimono Sleeve Leather Jacket

Sharp and dramatic yet classic and clever, this coat is an outfit-making piece—and not just because it's so oversized. Pop it on over a glamorous dress—or wear it during the day with a mini or shorts and you won't have to give your outfit a second thought.
