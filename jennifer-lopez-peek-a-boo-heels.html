Jennifer Lopez Wears Peek-a-Boo Heels on 'Marry Me' Press Tour

Publish date: 2024-10-03

It's another fabulous day for celebrity street style, courtesy of Jennifer Lopez's Marry Me press tour.

The multi-hyphenate has been making the rounds in New York City while promoting her forthcoming rom-com. Some of her most bold looks so far include a marbled coat by Fendi to an all-snakeskin ensemble by Roberto Cavalli.

Most recently, Lopez was spotted earlier today in Midtown in a classy outfit that channeled Old Hollywood glamor–with a twist.

The actress looked elegant in a black belted coat dress layered over a speckled gray collared top. But it was her shoes that stole the show: a pair of black pointed pumps with criss-crossing buckle straps and a prominent peek-a-boo feature that showed off a nude pedicure.

Other accessories included a pair of cat-eye shades, glittering diamond earrings, and a small black handbag.

The night before, J. Lo made yet another fashion statement via a bold coat during an appearance with boyfriend Ben Affleck.

For the outing, she wore a slouchy oversized fur coat from Coach's fall 2021 collection layered over a black midi dress featuring lace and a high leg slit. She accessorized with with the towering platform Junnie Sandals from her DSW collaboration and a quilted Coach shoulder bag.

Robert Kamau//Getty Images

Gotham//Getty Images

J. Lo is known to rep the luxury fashion house, having recently starred in Coach's spring 2022 campaign, along with Megan Thee Stallion, Noah Beck, and others. Last year, she also recreated an iconic scene from her 2002 "All I Have" music video, in which walked down a wintry New York City sidewalk while carrying multiple Coach bags.
