Who is Joe Pesci?

Joseph Frank Pesci is a retired American actor and musician. He is known for portraying harsh and volatile characters in various genres. He was born on February 9th, 1943, in Newark, New Jersey.

Joseph is the son of Maria and Angelo Pesci. His mother worked as a part-time barber, while his father was a forklift driver for General Motors and a bartender.

Acting profession

In 1990, Joe Pesci won the Oscar for Best Supporting Actor for his role as Tommy Devito in Goodfellas. He is also best known for his role as the conman Leo Getz in Lethal weapon 2.
