Publish date: 2024-06-28

Taylor Mills Finally exchanged vows with his fiance Brett Young!

Taylor, age 31, got married to his longtime love, Brett, age 37, on 3 November 2018 in a ceremony attended by 200 peoples; friends and family members, in Palm Springs, California.

In a post-wedding interview with People (4 November 2018), Mills told;

"We chose people in terms of performers that are really important to us and are close friends,"

Whereas, Brett added,

“This is the beginning of our life together, and I’m excited to say husband and wife!”

 Several guests attended and performed at the ceremony; Lee Brice sang I Don't Dance and Gavin DeGraw sang More Than Anyone.

Happily Married: Taylor Mills and Brett Young pose in their wedding threads (Published on 4 November 2018)

The duo had been dating for more than six years since their first meet at Arizona while Taylor was at school at ASU, four years before their dating life. 

In the early stage of their relationship, the couple split when Brett had to move to Nashville to pursue his country music career.
