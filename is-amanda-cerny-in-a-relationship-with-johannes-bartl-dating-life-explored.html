Amanda Cerny is an American YouTuber and a fitness professional. She is known for having once dated Logan Paul.

Cerny is an active philanthropist and UN Ambassador who has worked for non-profit organizations and charitable funds. She has been connected with previous and ongoing relief efforts in Haiti and Puerto Rico.
