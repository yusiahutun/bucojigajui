Publish date: 2024-08-26

Diptyque Fleur de Cerisier Candle

Diptyque Fleur de Cerisier Candle

To serious beauty devotees, the arrival of Diptyque's annual Fleur de Cerisier scented candle signals the start of spring.

A powdery, fruity blend of berries and musk, this fresh, uplifting scent is an ode to the fleeting beauty of the cherry blossom tree. Don't wait to snap one up: this ephemeral creation will be gone by the time those pale-pink petals begin to fall.

  • Burn time: 60 hours
  • Olfactory family: white floral
  • Wax material: a mineral-wax blend

Trudon Versailles Candle

Trudon Versailles Candle

The world's oldest candlemakers are delving into the rich history of 18th century Paris once more this spring.

Inspired by Louis XIV's famously indulgent celebrations at the Royal residency, Versaille is a refreshing recreation of the palace's abundant gardens, blending crisp mint and thyme with petal-fresh flowers, before revealing a honey-sweetened oak base. It's a bright, spring-appropriate ode to the brand's opulent beginnings.

  • Burn time: 60 hours
  • Olfactory family: floral
  • Wax material: vegetable

D.S. & Durga Dream Lychee Candle

D.S. & Durga Dream Lychee Candle

Now 10% Off

Cao Xueqin's 18th Century novel, Dream of the Red Chamber, and the rich ancient Chinese landscapes it brings to life, inspired D.S & Durga's latest fragrance. Bringing that aristocratic grandeur to life, this bright, juicy aroma is a hyper-realistic combination of tart lychee, rosewater and lily of the valley. It's refreshing, almost aquatic, and note-perfect for spring.

  • Burn time: 60 hours
  • Olfactory family: floral, fruity
  • Wax material: soy and vegetable

Perfumer H Ink Handblown Refillable Scented Candle

Revered perfumer Lyn Harris pays homage to the aromas of everyday life in her eponymous fragrance line. Byredo Sweet Grass Candle

Byredo Sweet Grass Candle

Byredo's cult candles are loved for more than their minimalist aesthetic. Exemplifying the brand's talent for creating unusual, addictive home scents, Sweet Grass is a mysterious blend of sage and palo santo, layered over a comfort blanket of soft ambroxan and musk.

  • Burn time: 60 hours
  • Olfactory family: green
  • Wax material: varies by scent; but largely vegetable wax

Penhaligon's Ceylon Pekoe Candle

Penhaligon's Ceylon Pekoe Candle

Trust Penhaligon's to create a candle line that boasts truly unique, memorable scents. Each one delivers olfactory escapism, but Ceylon Pekoe is a real stand-out: imagine earthy tea and spiced cardamom with the heady sweetness of lily of the valley.

  • Burn time: 30 hours
  • Olfactory family: white floral

Loewe Roasted Hazelnut Candle

Loewe Roasted Hazelnut Candle

Champagne and Smoke Filthy Rose Candle

The duo have used the maximum level of fragrance oils possible, so expect it to fill your entire home with its heady trail.

Dior 30 Montaigne Giant Candle

Dior 30 Montaigne Giant Candle

Home fragrance does not come more luxurious than Dior's gigantic, centrepiece-worthy candle. The white porcelain votive – which is heavy enough to require two hands – sits atop its own silver metal base, and comes protected by a matching lid.

The stand-out scent has to be 30 Montaigne: a history-steeped ode to the designer's first maison, rich with notes of amber, woods and spice.

  • Burn time: 110 hours
  • Olfactory family: spice, amber
  • Wax material: plant-based

Carriere Freres The Scented Taper Candles in Spearmint

Carriere Freres The Scented Taper Candles in Spearmint

Reconnecting to its rich history, Carrière Frères has launched a collection of taper candles, crafted from pure vegetable wax and scented with fresh, single-note fragrances that won't feel out of place in a kitchen or dining room.

Cleverly, they only give off their fragrance when cold: light the wick at dinner time, and they'll deliver nothing but a halo of light, providing the perfect backdrop to an evening of entertaining.

  • Burn time: 10 hours each
  • Olfactory family: herbaceous, green
  • Wax material: vegetable

Best sustainable candle

Haeckels Pluviophile Candle

Best sustainable candle

Haeckels Pluviophile Candle

Credit: Selfridges

As we increasingly seek to bring nature back into our hyper-connected lives, home fragrance brands are turning to the outside world to inspire invigorating scents.

Wet concrete might not be a scent that ranks highly on your list of favourites, but Haeckels' Pluviophile candle is strangely soothing. It's blended with petrichor – the steely, ozonic scent compound released after a downpour – with moss and citrus to conjure a rainy seaside day with spooky accuracy.

This being Haeckels, the production is especially thoughtful too: expect recyclable and compostable packaging, and an insulated glass vessel destined to be reborn as a tea or cocktail glass.

  • Burn time: 40 hours
  • Olfactory family: aquatic
  • Wax material: soy

Granado Verbena Scented Candle

Granado Verbena Scented Candle

  • Burn time: 50 hours
