About Goethe-institut

“Communication with the world. For diversity, understanding and trust.” Such are the simple words that greet you once you visit the “About Us” page of the Goethe-institut Nairobi.

The renowned German learning centre prides itself on promoting cultural exchange, education, and societal discourse in an international context. On top of this, the institution supports teaching and learning of the German language.

Goethe-Institut is at No.3, Monrovia Street, Maendeleo House, Nairobi. The learning centre’s central location, at the heart of the Nairobi City, makes it easily accessible to people willing to expand their knowledge of the German language and learn the culture.

Goethe-institut Nairobi courses

To partake in the Goethe-institut Nairobi intake, you must first know the course you want to apply to at the institution. It is important to note that besides the German courses offered, the school provides German exams and services related to teaching German (advice and services and professional development). So, what are the courses offered?

Online German courses

Goethe-institut has an online learning portal where students experience independent learning, including live sessions with a teacher. The online learning portal also allows to partake in group and individual courses.

The online course is for people who can read, write, listen, and speak German. The courses are great for beginners as well as advanced learners. You can choose:

  • Online group courses: You get individual learning, support from your teacher and a modern online learning platform.
  • Online individual courses: This allows you access to personal learning, 50% live sessions, 50% self-study, and modern online learning.
  • Blended learning: This module provides access to independent study, 50% live sessions in a group, 50% self-study, and a modern online learning platform.

All you need to do to access these online courses is to take a placement test, book, prepare, and start the course. Once you book, you will receive the login details through email before the course begins.

Intensive and superintensive courses

The intensive and superintensive courses allow you to learn German with professionals. However, you must first assess your ability so the learning institution can choose the appropriate course level. People who have prior knowledge of German start at level A1. The intensive courses include:

  • A1-A2: 132 units for 11 weeks.
  • B1: 150 units for 12 weeks.
  • B2.1 and B2.2: 90 units for six weeks
  • C1-C2: 120 units for 12 weeks.

The super intensive course consists of A1-A2. These are 132 units of 45 minutes each that last for only six weeks.

Courses for children and teenagers

Children and teenagers are included at Goethe-institut. They can actively experience the language in role-playing games, interviews and projects. All classes are customised for the different age groups. There are levels A1.1, A1.2, and A2.2, B1.1, and B1.2. These classes ordinarily start in January, May and September and last 12 weeks.

Exam preparation courses

These informative courses prepare you for the internationally recognised exams of the Goethe-institut.

Specialised courses

You can access an extensive range of specialised courses on various topics at Goethe-institut. These courses take place on the weekends.
