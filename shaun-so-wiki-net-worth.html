Early Life of Shaun So

Shaun So was born in 1980, in the USA. He is currently 39 years. His nationality is American-Chinese and his ethnicity is Asian. Shaun joined the University of Chicago in 1999 where he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Chicago in Political Science with Honors from 1999-to 2003.

He studied Mandarin at Tsinghua University in 2001 and holds a Master of Arts (MBA) from the City University of New York.

Who is Shaun’s So Wife? How many Children did They Share?
