I have 38G boobs but I still wear crop tops

Publish date: 2024-04-14

For women with fuller busts, finding a crop top that fits can often be a frustrating struggle. 

This is why a TikToker from Virginia known as Curvy Amanda has taken it upon herself to find the perfect fit for her voluptuous frame.

The influencer, who is 5'2 and weighs 230lbs counts more than 368,300 followers on TikTok, has opened up about her quest to find the best tops to fit her US size 38G-J.

In a video shared on her social media, she recommended a top costing $31.99 from Amazon, revealing how flattering the form-fitting garment looked on her frame. 

Viewers with similarly large busts were thankful for her recommendation, admitted that they also had struggled to find cropped tops that fit them well.

Amanda tried on the top in a video to show her followers exactly how it fitted her. 

'I love to find a crop top that covers everything,' Amanda said, showing off the garment.

'I want them to actually hit at my waist and not right under my bust,' she explained, adding: 'This is perfect.'

'I love getting a pop of color or neutrals so I can mix it up depending on my mood for the day.'
