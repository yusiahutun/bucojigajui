This seashell-shaped Airbnb could be yours for 238 a night

Publish date: 2024-08-06

If you're looking for a magical and out-of-the-ordinary holiday experience this summer, then Isla Mujeres in Mexico is the place to be. The stunning island is home to an incredible ocean-themed property dubbed the Shell House – and it will make all of your childhood dreams come true.

MORE: See the latest travel features here

Appearing like something straight out of The Little Mermaid, the world-famous house is shaped like two white seashells, with the magical mermaid theme reflected throughout. From the shell-shaped headboards on the beds to the seashell ornaments, every inch of the home has been designed to make you feel like you've stepped into your very own underwater palace, all while offering stunning views across to the Caribbean Ocean nearby.

The bedroom at the Shell House in Mexico

The home was designed by architects Raquel and Eduardo Ocampo, who now act as hosts to guests who have travelled from all over the world to stay in their iconic house, which is available to rent out via Airbnb. It can be yours from £238 per night for four people – around £59 each per night - and we think it's well worth shelling out for.

MORE: Get inspiration from celebrity holidays here

As well as being arguably one of the most Instagrammable homes in the world, the Shell House has impressive facilities of its own including a large private swimming pool and outdoor terrace, two king beds, a kitchenette and BBQ area. You may have to get used to outside attention though; the owners warn that tourists often stop by to admire the impressive architecture, but who can blame them!

The swimming pool at the Shell House

Fancy booking a visit? The Shell House is located on the north side of Isla Mujeres, off the coast of Mexico. The best way to get there from the UK is to fly to Cancun, then get a ferry across from Puerto Cancun to Isla Mujeres, which takes just 15 minutes. We're already planning our visit!
