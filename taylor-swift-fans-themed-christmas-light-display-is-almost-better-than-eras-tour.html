Taylor Swift fans themed Christmas light display is almost better than Eras Tour

Publish date: 2024-02-19

Taylor Swift needs to watch out as the hitmaker’s fans are bringing out outstanding light and sound productions taking inspiration from the singer’s artistry and stage designs.

This holiday season is bringing out the most treasured sentiments of Swifties as they dedicate videos paying tribute to the mastery of Taylor Swift. A single mom’s video about Taylor Swift making this year ‘the best ever’ as she made new memories with her daughter moved the music legend recently.

Another user created a light and sound show in their front yard with chosen Tay Tay tracks for their toddler Swiftie.

Taylor Swift fan pays tribute with an epic display of light and sound

Taking inspiration from the Grammy-winning celebrity, a Taylor Swift fan has created the most epic light and sound production for Christmas. The TikTok user Tom BetGeorge shared: “My toddler and I love Taylor Swift so this was a no-brainer. Here’s a medley I made with 11 of our favorite Taylor Swift songs.”

The song medley includes some of Tay Tay’s most popular tracks including Shake It Off, Cruel Summer, 22, Bad Blood, and more. Fans have compared the show with actually attending Taylor Swift’s concert. A fan gushed over the attention to detail writing: “This wasn’t just a Christmas light show, this was a production! From the music mesh to the lights, to the pyrotechnics! Even the the videography! Well done! Well done!”

Another fan demanded: “We need Taylor performing in the front yard with the light show! Awesome job!”

Taylor Swift’s ‘Christmas magic’

For Swifties, Christmas is extra emotional as they believe, it is the season when the Tay magic started. In 2019, the songstress released a holiday special song Christmas Tree Farm with a video that showed a baby Tay Tay unveiling one of her first guitars on Christmas Eve.

The video is very significant for fans as one says: “I actually didn’t tear up, until I saw that little clip of her opening a guitar as a gift. Baby Taylor didn’t know at the time, just how influential a guitar would be in her life!”
