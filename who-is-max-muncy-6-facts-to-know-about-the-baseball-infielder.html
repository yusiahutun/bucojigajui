Who is Max Muncy?

Max Muncy, formally referred to as Maxwell Steven Muncy, was born on August 25, 1995, in Watauga, Texas, USA. He grew up in Keller High School in Keller, Texas, where he began honing his baseball skills before going on to Baylor University, where he also played baseball for the Baylor Bears baseball team from 2010 to 2012.

After graduating from college and having already decided to pursue a career as a professional baseball player, Max was selected by the Oakland Athletics as number 169 in the fifth round of the 2012 Major League Baseball (MLB) draft.
