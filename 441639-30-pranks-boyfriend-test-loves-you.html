30 pranks to do on your boyfriend to test how much he loves you

Publish date: 2024-03-19

Couples that prank together stay together. There's nothing wrong with plotting a hilarious prank on your boyfriend. The list of pranks you can attempt to pull on your boyfriend is endless, and they will range from simple to elaborate. If you are stuck on ideas, pranks to do on your boyfriend should come in handy to inspire you.

A prank is a joke that you play on someone to trick them. Some require nothing more than your cheeky face, while others need a bit more planning and a few supplies to pull off. Most people have the habit of playing pranks on April Fools Day. You and your boyfriend should laugh together on the other days of the year.

Pranks to do on your boyfriend over text

Playing pranks over text is a good way to keep your boyfriend engaged and bring a smile to his face when he is busy or feeling low. If you are planning on pranking your boyfriend but don't know how to do it, then these pranks to do on your boyfriend over the phone should come in handy.

1. I can't believe you lied to me

You can send a text telling him that you couldn't believe he could lie to you like that, and if you want to add more to the fun, tell him you want to break up. He will ask you several questions and try to defend himself.

2. I don't know you

When he sends you a text, try to pretend that you are someone else. Pretend that you don't know him and have never heard his name. If he tries to inquire about your identity, give him a different name. If you notice that he is asking so many questions and seems confused, you have pulled off the prank.

3. I have been fired

You can send him a text telling him that you have issues at your workplace and have been fired. He will sympathize with you and do everything within his power to calm and console you. If your boyfriend is so caring, he will even send a box of chocolates to show that he cares.

4. There is an intruder in my apartment

You can send him a text message informing him that an intruder in your apartment or someone has broken into your apartment. Express how scared you are and before you know it, you will see him drive into the parking lot. This prank will test how much he cares for you.

5. I bought the latest iPhone for you

Everyone wants to have the latest iPhone, and your boyfriend is no exception. You can send him a text message telling him that you have bought the latest release, and he will be so excited. It is important to note that this prank will leave him feeling disappointed when he discovers it was just a prank.

6. My parents are visiting

Send a text message to your boyfriend informing him that your parents will be coming to visit and they will be staying for one month. This prank will leave him speechless as he might not be prepared to host your parents.

Pranks to do on your boyfriend online

If you want to mess with your boyfriend's day, tampering with his social media is the way to do it. Sneaky pranks are fun, harmless ways to cause social media mayhem.

1. Sending a weird picture on Instagram or Facebook

Tell your boyfriend that you accidentally posted a nude photo of him on Instagram or Facebook, and close friends and family members have already viewed the photo. The prank will throw him off guard, and he will be a little irked before he realizes it was just a joke.

2. I have not been honest with you

Send him a message telling him that you have not been honest with him for some time and you were afraid of telling him the truth because you do not want to lose him. Your boyfriend will send so many chats but ignore them and don't reply for an hour. When playing this prank, you need to exercise caution because it could ruin your relationship, especially if you don't trust each other.

3. Change his birth date on Twitter and Facebook

You can steal your boyfriend's phone and adjust his birthday date on Twitter or Facebook to tomorrow. He will be confused when he starts receiving birthday messages and wishes from friends and colleagues.

4. You have 500 notifications

You can take your boyfriend's phone while he is asleep and go to his Facebook profile. Like every single thing that has ever been posted. When he wakes up in the morning and checks his Facebook page, he will find hundreds of notifications waiting, and he will be bombarded with alerts.

5. Good news!

On your social media page, post a status congratulating your boyfriend for his new job and tag his account so that it shows up on his profile. He will get swarmed with stunned, congratulatory messages.

6. Influx of new friends

You can friend request everyone in your boyfriend's list of people you may know. The feed refreshes endlessly, and you will keep sending friend requests to many people. Your boyfriend will be baffled by the influx of new friends over the next few days.

Romantic pranks for boyfriend

Relationships require effort to make them work. But time and again, injecting romantic pranks spices things up for a couple. So here are some cute pranks to pull on your boyfriend.

1. I crashed your car

If your boyfriend loves his car more than anything else in this world, this is the best prank you can pull on him. Pretend that your car is at your service centre and that you want to go to the grocery store. Borrow his car for a few hours. Then, go back to where he is in an agitated manner and tell him that you have crashed his car.

2. Marker pen madness

You should wait until your boyfriend falls asleep. Pick a marker pen and draw something cheekier on his face. When he wakes up and looks in the mirror, the reaction on his face will leave you in stitches.

3. Tell him to call you urgently and ask a silly question

Act like you are in some trouble and ask him to call you urgently. When he calls back, don't answer the phone immediately. After he picks up the phone, you can ask a silly question.

4. Leave a surprise on his bed

Find something scary such as a plastic snake or spider, and place it on the pillow just before he wakes up in the morning. You can film his reaction when he wakes up to the scary creature on the pillow only to realize later that it is a toy.

5. Cover the tops of his hygiene products

If your boyfriend loves using hygiene products such as shaving gel, body wash, or face wash, you can pull this prank on him. Unscrew the tops of all the bottles and tubes, and cover them using plastic wrap. Put the tops back on, and he will have no idea why nothing is coming out.

6. Manicure and pedicure?

If your boyfriend is a heavy sleeper, you can pull this prank on him. Wait until he is deep asleep and paint all his nails. After he wakes up, wait for him to notice something different about his hands and feet. Look out for the reaction on his face.

Funny pranks to do on your boyfriend

There's just something hilarious about playing pranks on boyfriend in person. You get to see the magic unfold right in front of your eyes. These funny pranks will ensure that you break into fits of laughter once you are done playing pranks.

1. Beach trap

The next time you go to the beach with your boyfriend, place a piece of cloth on the sand and sit on it for a drink. When he leaves for the toilet, remove the fabric and dig a big hole hurriedly under it. Then, place the sheet back on the hole neatly. When he comes back and sits down, he will slide down in the hole, and you will have the laugh of your life.

2. Give him a ghost scare

You can buy a mask that has a ghost face from the stores. When he falls deep asleep, arrange the pillows in a manner that shows that someone is sleeping close to him. Place the mask facing him. Hide somewhere in the corner and make noises to wake him up. When he wakes up sees the ghost face, he will jump out of bed feeling scared.

3. Replace the hand wash with sticky glue

You can replace the hand wash with sticky glue. Add food colour to it so that it resembles the original hand wash. When your boyfriend comes to the sink to wash his hands, he will feel a sense of befuddlement.

4. Creating a floury mess

You can take some flour and put it in a small plastic jar. Take the jar and place it on edge at the top of the door. Ensure that you leave the door slightly open. When your boyfriend enters the room and moves the door, his head and face will be covered in flour.

5. Open the hinges of the bathroom door

Open the hinges of the bathroom door. If your boyfriend needs to use the bathroom door, he will enter the room and close the door. The door will come out, and he will be confused.

6. Swapping vinegar with soda

If your boyfriend loves drinking soda, you can replace the contents of the soda bottles in your fridge with vinegar. When your boyfriend opens the soda bottle and drinks it, the expression on his face will leave you in stitches.

Pranks to make your boyfriend mad

Doing a good prank on your boyfriend is a fun way of showing him a different side to your personality, especially if he thinks you're a sweet and nice girl. So here are some pranks that will make him feel mad.

1. The relationship is not working

If you are madly in love with each other, you can tell him that you are tired of the relationship and want to break up with him. Tell him that things are not working and you think the best option is to end the relationship. This prank will hit him hard, and he will be so mad at you. He will try to question what went wrong.

2. I am pregnant

If you and your boyfriend are not planning to have any kids in the near future, you can send him a message telling him that you are pregnant. You can fake the pregnancy test at home to make it more believable. Your boyfriend will be so mad because he is not ready to become a father. After he freaks out for a few minutes, tell him that you were kidding.

3. Reset the clock of his phone to one hour before

At night, you can change the clock settings on his phone to one hour before. When he goes to sleep, he will wake up earlier than usual and report to his office. He might find himself alone in the office because he has reported one hour earlier than expected. This prank will make him mad and furious, and he will not believe that you did this to him.

4. Destroy his iPod

Guys love their iPods more than anything in this world. You can find a cheap replica of his iPod from the store and smash it completely. You can steal his iPod and hide it. Hand him the smashed iPod and tell him that you are sorry that you destroyed his iPod. He will freak out. Afterwards, you can hand him his real iPhone and tell him it was just a prank.

5. The remote is not working

If your boyfriend loves watching sports or movies on television and often takes the remote away, you can get back at him by playing this prank. You can take a piece of scotch tape matching the remote and dot it with black colour. Place it over the sensor of the remote. When he tries to operate the remote, it will not work. He will even try to change the batteries, but it will not work.

6. Simulated spill on the laptop

Prepare a fake spill by mixing glue with colour paint and place it on a vessel of your choice. Turn the vessel on the side to allow the contents to spill over onto a plastic bag. Once the mixture is dry, you can transfer it to the laptop keyboard.

These pranks to do on your boyfriend is the most important tool that you have at your disposal if you want to spice up your relationship. These will go a long way in strengthening the bond between you and your boyfriend while at the same time adding excitement and humour to the relationship.
