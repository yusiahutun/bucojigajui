Reports: Kadyn Proctor to leave Iowa, return to Alabama

Publish date: 2024-06-17

Offensive lineman Kadyn Proctor will enter the transfer portal, with his sights set on Alabama, The Athletic’s Scott Dochterman first reported. He announced his transfer to Iowa from Alabama less than two months ago.

The Proctor saga has been a murky one to say the least. He committed to Iowa as a recruit, then flipped his commitment to Alabama because “he didn’t want to settle.” Then after one season in which he started every game, he returned to the state he grew up in.
